# Investment Strategy - SKILL.md

## Core Principles
- **Risk Management**: Preserve capital first, grow second
- **Diversification**: Spread across asset classes, sectors, geographies
- **Time Horizon**: Match investments to specific goals (short/medium/long-term)
- **Cost Awareness**: Minimize fees, taxes, and transaction costs

## Key Strategies
1. **Asset Allocation**: Primary driver of returns (stocks/bonds/cash/alternatives)
2. **Rebalancing**: Regular portfolio adjustments to maintain target allocations
3. **Dollar-Cost Averaging**: Consistent investments regardless of market conditions
4. **Quality Focus**: Invest in fundamentally strong companies/assets

## Risk Assessment
- Understand personal risk tolerance
- Consider liquidity needs
- Account for market cycles and economic conditions
- Stress test portfolios for various scenarios

## Decision Framework
1. Define investment objectives
2. Establish measurable benchmarks
3. Implement strategy systematically
