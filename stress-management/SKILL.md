## Core Principles
- Recognize stress signals early (physical, emotional, cognitive)
- Differentiate between controllable and uncontrollable stressors
- Prioritize recovery as part of performance

## Quick Practices
- **Breathing**: 4-7-8 technique (inhale 4s, hold 7s, exhale 8s)
- **Grounding**: 5-4-3-2-1 method (notice 5 things you see, 4 feel, 3 hear, 2 smell, 1 taste)
- **Movement**: 5-minute walk or stretch break
- **Reframe**: Ask "Will this matter in a week? A year?"

## Prevention
- Schedule downtime like appointments
- Set clear boundaries (work/off hours, notification limits)
- Maintain sleep, nutrition, hydration basics
- Identify one daily "stress buffer" activity
