## Executive Summary
Quality Control (QC) is a systematic process ensuring products or services meet specified requirements and customer expectations. This skill encompasses inspection, testing, measurement, and corrective action procedures to maintain consistent quality standards throughout production and delivery cycles. Effective QC reduces waste, prevents defects, enhances customer satisfaction, and protects brand reputation while ensuring regulatory compliance.

## Core Components

### 1. Quality Standards & Specifications
- **Establishment:** Define measurable quality parameters (dimensions, materials, performance)
- **Documentation:** Create detailed quality manuals, work instructions, and acceptance criteria
- **Benchmarking:** Compare against industry standards (ISO, ASTM, ANSI) and competitor products
- **Customer Requirements:** Translate customer needs into technical specifications

### 2. Inspection & Testing Procedures
- **Incoming Materials:** Verify raw materials/components against purchase specifications
- **In-Process:** Monitor production at critical control points
- **Final Inspection:** Comprehensive evaluation before release
- **Sampling Plans:** Determine sample sizes using statistical methods (AQL, MIL-STD-105E)
- **Non-Destructive Testing:** Visual, ultrasonic, radiographic, magnetic particle inspection
- **Destructive Testing:** Stress tests, fatigue tests, chemical analysis

### 3. Measurement Systems Analysis
- **Calibration:** Regular calibration of measurement equipment
- **Gauge R&R:** Repeatability & Reproducibility studies
- **Measurement Uncertainty:** Quantifying potential error in measurements

### 4. Defect Management
- **Classification:** Categorize defects by severity (Critical, Major, Minor)
- **Documentation:** Record defects with photos, measurements, and conditions
- **Containment:** Immediate isolation of non-conforming products
- **Root Cause Analysis:** Fishbone diagrams, 5 Whys, Pareto analysis
- **Corrective Actions:** Implement and verify effectiveness of solutions
