# SKILL: Booking

## Overview
The Booking skill enables users to schedule appointments, make reservations, or book services through natural language requests. This skill parses temporal information, identifies booking intent, and extracts key details to facilitate scheduling actions.

## Core Capabilities
- **Appointment Scheduling**: Book meetings, consultations, or professional services
- **Reservation Management**: Make restaurant, hotel, or venue reservations
- **Service Booking**: Schedule deliveries, maintenance, or other services
- **Time/Date Parsing**: Extract and interpret temporal information from natural language
- **Duration Detection**: Identify meeting length or service duration requirements
- **Participant Management**: Handle single or multi-person bookings
