## Purpose
To create clear, actionable briefs that align stakeholders and empower writers to produce effective copy.

## Core Principles

**Clarity Over Creativity**
- Briefs are functional documents, not creative showcases
- Every element must serve a practical purpose
- Ambiguity is the enemy

**Strategic Alignment**
- Connect copy to business objectives
- Define what success looks like (metrics)
- Identify constraints and guardrails

**Writer-Centric Design**
- Provide what writers need, not everything you know
- Answer questions before they're asked
- Enable autonomy within boundaries
