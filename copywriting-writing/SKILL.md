## Purpose
To translate business objectives into clear, actionable creative direction that enables effective copy development.

## Core Competencies

**Strategic Translation**
- Extract key insights from stakeholders
- Identify target audience needs and pain points
- Align copy objectives with business goals
- Define success metrics and KPIs

**Brief Structuring**
- Craft compelling creative challenges
- Define clear deliverables and formats
- Establish tone, voice, and brand guidelines
- Specify technical requirements and constraints

**Audience Definition**
- Develop detailed audience personas
- Map customer journeys and touchpoints
- Identify key messaging triggers
- Address audience objections proactively
