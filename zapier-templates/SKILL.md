## Purpose
Quickly create and share reusable Zapier automation workflows using pre-built templates.

## Key Actions
- **Browse Templates:** Search by app, use case, or category
- **Use Template:** Click \"Use this Zap\" to copy into your account
- **Customize:** Modify triggers, actions, and settings for your needs
- **Share:** Generate template links from existing Zaps

## Best Practices
- Start with templates close to your use case
- Always test with sample data first
- Check app availability in your Zapier plan
- Replace placeholder data with your actual info
- Review permissions before turning Zaps on

## Quick Start
1. Find template in Zapier Template Gallery
2. Click \"Use this Zap\"
3. Connect your app accounts
4. Customize steps as needed
5. Test and turn on
