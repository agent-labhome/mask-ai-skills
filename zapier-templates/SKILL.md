## Overview
Zapier Templates are pre-built automation workflows that connect apps and services to perform common tasks without requiring users to build Zaps from scratch. They provide ready-to-use solutions for popular use cases across various business functions.

## Core Concepts

### 1. **Template Structure**
- **Trigger**: The event that starts the automation (e.g., \"New email in Gmail\")
- **Action(s)**: What happens after the trigger (e.g., \"Create task in Asana\")
- **Optional**: Filters, Searches, Delays, Paths (conditional logic)

### 2. **Template Types**
- **Single-step**: One trigger → one action
- **Multi-step**: One trigger → multiple sequential actions
- **Multi-path**: Conditional workflows with branching logic

### 3. **Key Components**
- **App Connections**: Authentication setup for each service
- **Field Mapping**: Connecting data between apps
- **Testing**: Preview and validation before activation
- **Monitoring**: History log and error tracking
