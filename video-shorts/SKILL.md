# SKILL.md: Video Shorts

## Metadata

**Skill Name:** Video Shorts  
**Category:** Content Creation & Social Media  
**Difficulty:** Intermediate  
**Time Required:** 2-4 hours per short  
**Tools Required:** Smartphone with camera, video editing app (CapCut, InShot, Premiere Rush), tripod/stand, good lighting  
**Platforms:** YouTube Shorts, Instagram Reels, TikTok, Facebook Reels  
**Optimal Length:** 15-60 seconds  
**Key Metrics:** Watch time, engagement rate, shares, completion rate  

## Skill Overview

Video Shorts are vertical, short-form videos designed for maximum engagement on social platforms. Unlike traditional videos, shorts prioritize immediate hook, fast pacing, and platform-native editing styles to capture attention in crowded feeds.

## Core Principles

### 1. **Platform Psychology**
- First 3 seconds are critical for retention
- Vertical format (9:16 aspect ratio)
- Sound-on assumption (85% watch with sound)
- Scroll-stopping visuals required

### 2. **Content Types**
- **Educational:** Quick tutorials, life hacks, facts
- **Entertainment:** Skits, comedy, trends
- **Inspirational:** Motivational speeches, transformations
- **Behind-the-Scenes:** Raw, authentic moments
- **Interactive:** Polls, questions, duets
