# SKILL.md: Ecommerce Brief Development

## Skill Overview
**Skill Name:** Ecommerce Brief Development  
**Purpose:** Create comprehensive, actionable ecommerce project briefs that align business objectives with technical requirements for development teams.

## Core Competencies

- **Market Understanding:** Identify target audience, competitive landscape, and market positioning
- **Revenue Models:** Define monetization strategies (B2C, B2B, subscription, marketplace)
- **Business Goals:** Translate business objectives into technical requirements
- **Success Metrics:** Establish KPIs and measurement frameworks
