# SKILL: Local SEO

**Goal:** Increase visibility in local search results and drive foot traffic.

## Core Competencies
- **GBP Management:** Optimize Google Business Profile with accurate info, photos, posts, and reviews.
- **Local Citations:** Ensure consistent NAP (Name, Address, Phone) across directories.
- **On-Page Localization:** Optimize title tags, meta descriptions, and content with local keywords.
- **Local Link Building:** Acquire backlinks from relevant local websites and organizations.
- **Review Management:** Generate and respond to customer reviews.
- **Local Schema Markup:** Implement structured data (LocalBusiness, etc.) on website.

## Key Metrics
- Local pack rankings
- GBP insights (calls, direction requests, website clicks)
- Review quantity & rating
- Local organic traffic

## Quick Wins
1. Claim and fully complete your Google Business Profile.
2. Solicit 3-5 reviews from happy customers.
