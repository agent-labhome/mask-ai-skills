## Practical Guide to Local SEO

### 1. Foundation: Understanding Local SEO
Local SEO optimizes your online presence to attract more business from relevant local searches on Google and other search engines. Unlike traditional SEO, it focuses on geographically constrained searches.

**Key Differentiators:**
- **Geo-modifiers:** Searches containing \"near me,\" city/neighborhood names, or \"in [location]\"
- **Local Pack:** The map and business listings that appear for local searches
- **Proximity:** Physical distance between searcher and business heavily influences rankings
