# Communication Skills

**Core Principles**
- **Clarity:** Be concise, specific, and jargon-free.
- **Active Listening:** Focus fully, ask clarifying questions, and summarize.
- **Empathy:** Consider your audience's perspective and feelings.
- **Confidence:** Project assurance through tone, posture, and preparation.

**Key Practices**
- **Verbal:** Adjust tone and pace for your message.
- **Non-Verbal:** Maintain eye contact and use open body language.
- **Written:** Structure logically, proofread, and use clear formatting.
- **Feedback:** Give it constructively; receive it graciously.

**Quick Tips**
- Prepare and know your audience.
- Pause to think before responding.
- Choose the right channel (email, call, meeting).
- Confirm understanding to avoid miscommunication.
