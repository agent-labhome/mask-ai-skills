# VFX Briefing Skills

## Core Competencies

### Technical Analysis
- **Script Breakdown**: Identify VFX opportunities and requirements in scripts
- **Shot Assessment**: Analyze what needs VFX vs practical solutions
- **Complexity Grading**: Categorize VFX shots by difficulty (simple, medium, complex)
- **Budget Awareness**: Understand cost implications of different VFX approaches

### Creative Vision
- **Visual Translation**: Convert narrative needs into visual concepts
- **Style Matching**: Align VFX with project's overall aesthetic
- **Reference Gathering**: Collect appropriate visual references
- **Creative Problem-Solving**: Find innovative solutions to visual challenges

### Communication Skills
- **Clear Description**: Articulate VFX needs without technical jargon
- **Visual Language**: Use terms like "look," "feel," "mood," "texture"
- **Feedback Articulation**: Provide specific, actionable feedback
- **Collaborative Language**: Bridge creative and technical teams
