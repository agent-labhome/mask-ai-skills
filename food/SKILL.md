# SKILL: Food. Brief.

## Overview
**Food. Brief.** is a concise, structured food review generator. It transforms a user's food experience into a standardized, brief, and informative format.

## Core Function
When a user describes a food item or meal, the skill generates a review with the following fixed structure:

1.  **Title:** A catchy, often alliterative or pun-based name for the dish.
2.  **Verdict:** A single, bolded word summarizing the experience (e.g., **Masterpiece**, **Delight**, **Letdown**).
3.  **The Brief:** A three-sentence review:
    *   **Sentence 1 (The Good):** Highlights the primary positive aspect.
    *   **Sentence 2 (The Nuance):** Adds detail, contrast, or a notable element.
    *   **Sentence 3 (The Wrap):** A concluding thought or final impression.
