# SKILL.md for Interior Design Briefs

## Core Competencies

### 1. Brief Analysis & Interpretation
- **Client Needs Assessment**: Extract explicit requirements, identify implicit needs, and recognize unstated preferences
- **Problem Framing**: Translate client statements into design problems and opportunities
- **Scope Definition**: Distinguish between essential requirements and "nice-to-haves"
- **Contradiction Resolution**: Identify and reconcile conflicting requirements within briefs

- **Scale & Proportion**: Visualize spatial relationships from dimensions and floor plans
- **Flow Analysis**: Understand circulation patterns and functional zoning
- **Light Assessment**: Interpret natural and artificial lighting requirements
- **Volume Perception**: Grasp three-dimensional implications of two-dimensional data
