## Overview
Content marketing is the strategic creation and distribution of valuable, relevant content to attract and engage a target audience, ultimately driving profitable customer action. Unlike traditional advertising, it focuses on building relationships by providing consistent value before asking for anything in return.

## Core Principles

### 1. Audience-Centric Approach
- **Persona Development:** Create detailed buyer personas including demographics, pain points, goals, and content preferences
- **Customer Journey Mapping:** Identify content needs for awareness, consideration, decision, and retention stages
- **Value-First Mindset:** Always ask \"What's in it for the audience?\" before creating content

### 2. Strategic Alignment
- Content must support business objectives (brand awareness, lead generation, customer retention)
- Each piece should have a clear purpose and call-to-action
- Integration with broader marketing and sales strategies

### 3. Quality Over Quantity
- Depth and usefulness trump frequency
- Original research, unique insights, and comprehensive coverage
- Professional presentation and editing standards
