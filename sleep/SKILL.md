## Overview

Sleep is a fundamental biological process essential for physical restoration, cognitive function, emotional regulation, and overall health. Unlike passive rest, sleep involves complex, active cycles that repair tissues, consolidate memories, and regulate hormones. Mastering sleep quality and consistency can transform energy levels, mental clarity, and long-term wellness.

## Detailed Guide

- **NREM (Non-Rapid Eye Movement)**: 75-80% of sleep

- **REM (Rapid Eye Movement)**: 20-25% of sleep

  - Memory consolidation, emotional processing

- Internal 24-hour clock regulated by the suprachiasmatic nucleus
- Light exposure is primary zeitgeber (time cue)
- Melatonin production begins 2-3 hours before natural bedtime
- Body temperature minimum occurs around 4-5 AM
