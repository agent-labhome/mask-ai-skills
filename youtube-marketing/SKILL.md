## Core Competency
Strategic planning, creation, optimization, and promotion of video content on YouTube to achieve business objectives including brand awareness, audience growth, lead generation, and revenue.

## Primary Objectives
- Increase channel subscribers and viewership
- Drive targeted traffic to websites or products
- Build brand authority and community
- Generate leads and sales
- Improve search visibility through video SEO

## Technical Skills Required

### 1. Content Strategy & Planning
- YouTube algorithm understanding (watch time, CTR, engagement)
- Audience research and persona development
- Content pillar and topic cluster development
- Video format selection (tutorials, reviews, vlogs, etc.)
- Content calendar and batch production planning

### 2. Video Production
- Basic videography principles (lighting, framing, audio)
- Scriptwriting for different video formats
- On-camera presentation skills (or directing talent)
