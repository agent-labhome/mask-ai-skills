## Overview
The **CRM Brief** skill enables users to generate comprehensive, structured briefs for Customer Relationship Management (CRM) system implementations, enhancements, or strategy development. This skill transforms high-level CRM needs into detailed, actionable briefs suitable for stakeholders, vendors, or internal teams.

## Core Capabilities

### 1. **Brief Generation**
- Creates structured CRM briefs from minimal input (e.g., \"We need a CRM for sales tracking\")
- Adapts output format based on audience (executive, technical team, vendor RFP)
- Includes all essential CRM brief components

### 2. **Component Coverage**
Automatically includes these standard sections in generated briefs:

**Project Overview**
- Business context and drivers
- Current state assessment
- Desired future state
- Success metrics and KPIs

**Functional Requirements**
- Sales pipeline management
- Marketing automation needs
- Customer service/support workflows
