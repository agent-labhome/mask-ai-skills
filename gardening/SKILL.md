## Core Abilities
- Plant selection and placement
- Soil preparation and amendment
- Watering and irrigation management
- Weed and pest control
- Pruning and maintenance
- Seasonal planning and succession planting

## Key Principles
- **Right plant, right place** – match plants to sunlight, soil, and climate
- **Healthy soil first** – focus on soil structure and nutrients
- **Observe and adapt** – gardens are dynamic ecosystems
- **Work with seasons** – timing is critical for planting and care

## Quick Tips
- Start small; expand as you learn
- Keep a garden journal
- Mulch to retain moisture and suppress weeds
- Group plants with similar water needs
- Attract pollinators with native flowering plants

## Tools
