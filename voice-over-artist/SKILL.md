## Core Competencies
- **Vocal Control:** Mastery of pitch, tone, pace, and breath for consistent delivery.
- **Script Interpretation:** Analyzing copy to understand audience, intent, and emotional tone.

- **Mic Technique:** Understanding proximity, plosive management, and consistent positioning.

## Key Deliverables
- Clean, edited audio files meeting specified format/bitrate.
- Multiple takes or pickups as requested.
- Clear, timely communication throughout the project.
