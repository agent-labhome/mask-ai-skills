# Startup Pitch

## Overview
The ability to effectively pitch your startup is a critical skill for fundraising, partnerships, and team building. This guide covers the structure, content, and delivery techniques for compelling pitches across different formats and audiences.

## Core Components

### 1. The Elevator Pitch (30-60 seconds)
- **Problem**: Clearly state the pain point
- **Solution**: Your product/service in simple terms
- **Market**: Size and target customers
- **Differentiation**: Why you're unique
- **Ask**: What you need (investment, partnership, etc.)
