# SKILL: Success. Brief.

## What It Is
A communication and thinking framework that forces clarity by requiring every message, update, or idea to be expressed in **three sentences or fewer**.

## Core Principle
If you can't explain it briefly, you don't understand it well enough. Brevity forces precision, eliminates noise, and respects the recipient's time and attention.

## How to Apply It

### 1. **The Three-Sentence Rule**
   - **First Sentence:** State the core conclusion, decision, or request.
   - **Second Sentence:** Provide the single most important reason, data point, or context.
   - **Third Sentence:** Specify the immediate next step or expected outcome.

### 2. **Before You Communicate**
   - Ask: "What is the ONE thing I need them to know/do?"
