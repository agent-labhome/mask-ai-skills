## Purpose
Transform high-level project ideas into structured, actionable roadmap briefs that align stakeholders and provide clear direction.

## Core Capabilities

### 1. **Strategic Structuring**
- Break down complex initiatives into logical phases
- Identify key milestones and deliverables
- Establish clear success metrics and KPIs
- Define scope boundaries and constraints

### 2. **Stakeholder Alignment**
- Identify all relevant stakeholders and their interests
- Map dependencies and inter-team requirements
- Clarify decision-making authority and escalation paths
- Balance competing priorities and resource constraints

### 3. **Risk & Dependency Management**
- Proactively identify potential risks and mitigation strategies
- Map critical dependencies (internal/external)
- Define contingency plans for high-risk items
- Establish monitoring and adjustment mechanisms
