## Core Competencies

**Theoretical Literacy**
- Understanding major paradigms (structural-functionalism, conflict theory, symbolic interactionism, feminist theory, critical race theory)
- Applying sociological imagination to connect personal experience to social structures
- Critiquing and comparing theoretical frameworks

**Research Methodology**
- Designing ethical research studies
- Quantitative analysis (survey design, statistical interpretation)
- Qualitative methods (interviews, ethnography, content analysis)
- Mixed-methods approaches
- Critical evaluation of research validity and bias

**Analytical Skills**
- Identifying patterns of social inequality (class, race, gender, sexuality)
- Analyzing social institutions (family, education, religion, economy, government)
- Deconstructing social construction of reality
- Examining power dynamics and social change
