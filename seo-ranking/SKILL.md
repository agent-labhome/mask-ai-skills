## Purpose
Quickly check a website's current Google search ranking for specific keywords.

## Commands
- `check ranking for [keyword]` - Get top 10 results for a keyword
- `find [website] ranking for [keyword]` - Check specific website's position
- `compare ranking for [keyword]` - See top competitors

## Output
- Top 10 Google results with positions
- Direct links to each result
- Position tracking for specific websites
- Quick competitive analysis
