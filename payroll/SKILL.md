## Overview
The Payroll skill enables users to manage employee payroll, calculate salaries, process payments, and handle payroll-related queries through natural language interactions.

## Core Capabilities

### 1. **Payroll Processing**
- Calculate gross and net salaries
- Process payroll for individuals or groups
- Handle different pay periods (weekly, bi-weekly, monthly)
- Generate payroll reports

### 2. **Deductions & Benefits Management**
- Calculate tax withholdings (federal, state, local)
- Process benefit deductions (health insurance, retirement)
- Handle voluntary deductions
- Manage garnishments and levies

### 3. **Employee Compensation**
- Handle different pay structures (hourly, salary, commission)
- Process overtime calculations
- Manage bonuses and incentives
- Track paid time off (PTO) accruals and usage

## 使用场景
本技能适用于需要帮助的用户，可应用于多种工作场景。
