## Overview
WooCommerce is a flexible, open-source e-commerce solution built on WordPress. It transforms WordPress sites into fully functional online stores with product management, shopping cart, checkout, and order processing capabilities.

## Core Competencies

- Shipping zone and method setup
- Email template customization
