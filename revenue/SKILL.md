## Purpose
Generate concise, data-driven revenue briefs that highlight key performance metrics, trends, and actionable insights for stakeholders.

## Core Capabilities
- **Data Synthesis**: Process revenue data from multiple sources (CRM, accounting software, spreadsheets)
- **Trend Analysis**: Identify patterns, growth rates, seasonality, and anomalies
- **Metric Calculation**: Compute standard revenue metrics (ARR, MRR, LTV, CAC, churn)
- **Comparative Analysis**: Period-over-period, year-over-year, vs. forecast comparisons
- **Insight Generation**: Surface meaningful takeaways from raw data
- **Visualization**: Create clear charts and graphs for key metrics
- **Stakeholder Tailoring**: Adapt depth and focus based on audience (executive vs. operational)

## Input Requirements
- Revenue data (transactions, subscriptions, contracts)
- Time periods for comparison
- Customer segmentation data (optional)
- Previous forecasts or targets (optional)
