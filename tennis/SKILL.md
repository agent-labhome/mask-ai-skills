## Overview
Tennis is a racket sport played individually against a single opponent (singles) or between two teams of two players each (doubles). Players use a strung racket to hit a hollow rubber ball covered with felt over a net and into the opponent's court.

## Core Skills

### Technical Skills
- **Strokes**
  - Forehand (dominant side groundstroke)
  - Backhand (one-handed or two-handed)
  - Volley (forehand and backhand)
  - Serve (flat, slice, kick/topspin)
  - Return of serve
  - Overhead smash
  - Drop shot
  - Lob (defensive and offensive)

- **Footwork**
  - Split step (timing movement with opponent's shot)
  - Side shuffle
  - Crossover steps
  - Recovery steps
  - Pivot turns
