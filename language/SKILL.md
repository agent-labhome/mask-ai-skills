## Language
**Brief**

### Core Competencies
- **Concise Communication**: Ability to distill complex ideas into essential points without losing meaning
- **Precision**: Selecting exact words that convey intended meaning without ambiguity
- **Clarity**: Structuring information for immediate comprehension
- **Audience Awareness**: Adapting language complexity and tone to the listener/reader
- **Efficiency**: Communicating maximum information with minimum words

### Technical Skills
- **Summarization**: Extracting key information from longer texts
- **Abstract Writing**: Creating executive summaries and overviews
- **Bullet Point Construction**: Organizing information in scannable formats
- **Terminology Management**: Knowing when to use technical terms vs. plain language
- **Editing for Brevity**: Removing redundancy and unnecessary qualifiers

### Applications
- Executive summaries and reports
- Meeting notes and action items
- Email communication
- Presentation abstracts
- Documentation overviews
