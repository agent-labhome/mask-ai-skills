# Series A Funding

## Overview
Series A is the first significant round of venture capital financing for a startup that has moved beyond the initial seed stage. At this stage, companies have typically validated their business model, demonstrated product-market fit, and are ready to scale operations.

## Key Characteristics
- **Purpose**: Transition from proving concepts to scaling the business
- **Investors**: Led by institutional venture capital firms, often with participation from previous seed investors
- **Dilution**: Founders typically give up 15-25% of equity
- **Structure**: Usually priced equity round (preferred stock)
