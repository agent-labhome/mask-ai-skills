## Purpose
Quickly capture, organize, and retrieve information in Notion.

## Core Commands
- **\"Add to Notion\"** - Create a new page or database entry
- **\"Find in Notion\"** - Search across your workspace
- **\"Update Notion\"** - Modify existing pages or properties
- **\"Show my Notion\"** - List recent pages or specific databases

## Best Practices
- Be specific with page titles and database names
- Use properties (tags, dates, status) for better organization
- Reference existing pages by their exact titles
- Keep notes concise for faster processing

## Examples
- \"Add meeting notes to Project X database\"
- \"Find the Q3 planning document\"
- \"Update status of 'Design Review' to Complete\"
- \"Show my tasks due this week\"

## Limitations
- Cannot edit complex database structures
