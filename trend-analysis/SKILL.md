## What is Trend Analysis?
Trend analysis is the systematic process of identifying, evaluating, and interpreting patterns in data over time to forecast future developments and inform strategic decisions. It involves examining historical data to detect consistent results or directional movements.

## Core Components

### 1. **Data Collection & Sources**
- **Quantitative Data**: Sales figures, website traffic, social media metrics, search volume
- **Qualitative Data**: Customer feedback, expert opinions, news articles, cultural observations
- **Sources**: Industry reports, government databases, academic journals, social listening tools

### 2. **Pattern Recognition**
- **Cyclical Trends**: Repeating patterns (seasonal, economic cycles)
- **Secular Trends**: Long-term directional movements
- **Fads vs. Trends**: Distinguishing temporary spikes from sustained movements
- **Correlation vs. Causation**: Identifying relationships without assuming direct causation

### 3. **Analysis Frameworks**
- **PESTLE Analysis**: Political, Economic, Social, Technological, Legal, Environmental factors
- **SWOT in Trend Context**: Strengths/Weaknesses/Opportunities/Threats related to emerging trends
- **Diffusion of Innovation**: How trends spread through populations (innovators → laggards)
