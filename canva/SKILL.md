# Canva SKILL.md

## Purpose
Quickly create professional designs for social media, presentations, documents, and marketing materials without design expertise.

## Key Capabilities
- **Templates**: Access thousands of pre-designed templates for all formats
- **Drag & Drop**: Intuitive visual editor with easy element placement
- **Brand Kit**: Maintain consistent colors, fonts, and logos
- **Collaboration**: Real-time editing and commenting with team members
- **Content Library**: Millions of stock photos, icons, illustrations, and videos
- **Export Options**: Download in PNG, JPG, PDF, MP4, and more

## Best For
- Social media graphics
- Presentations and slides
- Marketing materials (flyers, brochures)
- Basic video editing
- Team collaboration on visual content
- Quick design tasks without professional software
