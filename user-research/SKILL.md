## Purpose
A User Research Brief defines the scope, objectives, and approach for gathering insights about users to inform product decisions and validate assumptions.

## Core Components

**1. Project Context**
- Problem statement
- Business goals
- Key stakeholders

**2. Research Objectives**
- Primary questions to answer
- Success metrics
- Assumptions to validate

**3. Methodology**
- Research type (generative, evaluative, etc.)
- Techniques (interviews, surveys, usability tests)
- Participant criteria and recruitment plan

**4. Logistics**
- Timeline
- Resources needed
