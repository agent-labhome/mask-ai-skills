## Skill Description
Roadmap planning is the strategic process of defining, prioritizing, and communicating a product or initiative's vision, direction, and progress over time. It aligns teams, manages stakeholder expectations, and provides a flexible guide for execution while adapting to changing circumstances.

## Core Competencies

### 1. Vision & Strategy Translation
- Convert high-level vision into actionable themes and initiatives
- Align roadmap with organizational goals and OKRs
- Balance short-term delivery with long-term strategic objectives

### 2. Prioritization Frameworks
- Apply weighted scoring models (RICE, WSJF, Kano)
- Use effort vs. impact matrices
- Implement cost of delay analysis
- Balance stakeholder needs with user value

### 3. Stakeholder Management
- Map and understand stakeholder influence and interest
- Conduct effective roadmap reviews and feedback sessions
- Manage conflicting priorities diplomatically
- Communicate trade-offs clearly

### 4. Roadmap Visualization
- Create appropriate roadmap formats (now-next-later, timeline, theme-based)
- Tailor visualizations for different audiences (executives, engineers, customers)
- Maintain clarity while showing dependencies and relationships

### 5. Adaptive Planning
- Build flexibility for changing market conditions
- Establish review and adjustment rhythms
- Maintain backlog grooming discipline
- Implement feedback loops from execution
