# SKILL: HR Briefing

## Overview
The **HR Briefing** skill enables you to generate structured, professional HR briefs for various workplace scenarios. It transforms basic input into comprehensive HR documents suitable for management, employees, or policy development.

## When to Use
- Drafting HR policies or guidelines
- Preparing for employee meetings or disciplinary actions
- Creating onboarding/offboarding checklists
- Developing performance improvement plans
- Documenting workplace incidents or investigations
- Crafting internal communications about HR matters

## Input Requirements
Provide **at least one** of the following:
- **Scenario**: Description of the HR situation (e.g., "remote work policy update," "performance issue with an employee")
- **Key Elements**: Specific components to include (e.g., "confidentiality clause," "escalation process")
