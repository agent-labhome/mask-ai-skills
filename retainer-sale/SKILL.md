# Retainer Sale

## 功能
- 自动执行任务
- 提供专业建议
- 生成优化方案

## Core Objective
Convert project-based clients into ongoing monthly retainers by demonstrating the value of consistent partnership over transactional engagements.

## Key Principles
1. **Value-First**: Frame retainer as an investment in predictable results, not an expense
2. **Risk Mitigation**: Emphasize how retainers reduce project delays and communication gaps
3. **Partnership Mindset**: Position as collaborative relationship vs. vendor-client dynamic

## Essential Questions to Ask
- "What recurring challenges slow down your projects?"
- "How much time do you spend managing freelancers/vendors?"
- "What would consistent, prioritized access to our expertise be worth to your business?"

## Retainer Structure Elements
- **Scope**: Clearly define included services and response times
- **Flexibility**: Build in rollover or adjustment mechanisms
- **Outcomes**: Tie to business metrics, not just hours
