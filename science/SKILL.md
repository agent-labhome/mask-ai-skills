# SKILL.md: Science Briefing

## Core Competencies

### 1. Scientific Communication & Translation
- **Complex-to-Simple Translation**: Convert dense scientific literature, data, and concepts into clear, accessible language for non-specialist audiences
- **Jargon Elimination**: Identify and replace field-specific terminology with plain language equivalents while preserving accuracy
- **Audience Adaptation**: Tailor explanations to different knowledge levels (general public, policymakers, students, interdisciplinary researchers)

### 2. Information Synthesis & Distillation
- **Multi-Source Integration**: Synthesize information from research papers, preprints, conference proceedings, and expert commentary
- **Hierarchical Prioritization**: Identify and emphasize the most significant findings, implications, and limitations
- **Contextual Framing**: Place new findings within broader scientific context and historical development
