## Practical Framework: The SPEAK System

### 1. **Structure & Strategy** (The Blueprint)
**Purpose:** Create a clear, logical framework before you write a single word.
- **Audience Analysis:** Ask \"Who are they? What do they need? What will they do with this information?\"
- **Core Message:** Define one central idea you want them to remember (your \"Twitter takeaway\").
- **Classic Structures:**
    - **Problem-Solution:** Identify a pain point, then present your answer.
    - **Past-Present-Future:** Show evolution and trajectory.
    - **Three Points:** Simple, memorable, and effective (e.g., \"The three pillars of...\").
- **Rule of Three:** People remember information best in triplets.

### 2. **Preparation & Practice** (The Work)
**Purpose:** Transform anxiety into confidence through mastery of material.
