# SKILL: Partnerships

## Overview
The Partnerships skill enables the creation, management, and optimization of strategic alliances between organizations. This involves identifying potential partners, structuring mutually beneficial agreements, and maintaining productive long-term relationships to achieve shared objectives.

## Core Competencies

### 1. Strategic Alignment & Identification
- **Market & Ecosystem Analysis:** Map industry landscapes to identify potential partners (complementary businesses, adjacent services, channel partners, technology providers).
- **Strategic Fit Assessment:** Evaluate how a partnership aligns with organizational goals, brand values, and target audiences.
- **Value Proposition Development:** Articulate the mutual benefits for both parties (revenue sharing, co-marketing, product integration, market access, resource sharing).

### 2. Outreach & Relationship Initiation
- **Targeted Prospecting:** Identify and contact key decision-makers within potential partner organizations.
