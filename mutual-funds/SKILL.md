## Core Understanding
- **Definition:** Professionally managed investment pools that collect money from many investors to buy diversified portfolios of stocks, bonds, or other securities.
- **Key Purpose:** Provide diversification, professional management, and accessibility to various markets for individual investors.

## Essential Knowledge
- **Types:** Equity funds, bond funds, index funds, balanced funds, money market funds, sector-specific funds.
- **Key Metrics:** Net Asset Value (NAV), expense ratio, turnover rate, performance benchmarks.
- **Costs:** Loads (sales charges), management fees, 12b-1 fees (marketing/distribution).
- **Risk Profile:** Varies by fund type (equity funds generally higher risk than bond funds).
