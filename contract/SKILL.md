## Purpose
Create clear, concise legal contract summaries that capture essential terms and obligations for quick reference.

## Key Elements to Extract
- **Parties**: Identify all involved entities
- **Core Purpose**: Primary objective of the agreement
- **Key Terms**: Duration, payment terms, deliverables, milestones
- **Obligations**: Specific duties of each party
- **Termination**: Conditions for ending the agreement
- **Special Clauses**: Confidentiality, IP rights, liability, dispute resolution

## Format Guidelines
- Use bullet points for readability
- Bold key terms for emphasis
- Keep to one page maximum
- Use plain language, avoid legal jargon when possible
- Include reference to original document (date, version)

## Quality Checks
- Verify all parties are correctly identified
- Ensure no critical obligations are omitted
- Confirm dates and financial terms are accurate
- Check that special conditions are highlighted
