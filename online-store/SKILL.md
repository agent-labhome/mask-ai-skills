## Core Purpose
Build and manage a fully functional e-commerce platform with product listings, shopping cart, checkout, and order management.

## Core Capabilities

### 1. Product Management
- Create, edit, and delete product listings
- Manage product categories and tags
- Handle product variants (sizes, colors, etc.)
- Upload and organize product images
- Set pricing, inventory levels, and discounts

### 2. Shopping Experience
- Display products with filtering and sorting
- Implement search functionality
- Create product detail pages with descriptions and reviews
- Build shopping cart with add/remove/update functionality
- Save cart for returning customers

### 3. Checkout & Payment
- Multi-step checkout process
- Collect shipping and billing information
- Integrate payment gateways (Stripe, PayPal, etc.)
