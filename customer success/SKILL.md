## Core Competencies
- **Relationship Management**: Build and maintain trusted advisor relationships with key stakeholders.
- **Proactive Engagement**: Anticipate customer needs and initiate value-focused conversations.
- **Product Adoption**: Guide customers toward optimal usage and feature adoption.
- **Renewal & Expansion**: Drive contract renewals and identify growth opportunities.
- **Risk Mitigation**: Identify and address churn risks through data and direct feedback.

## Key Activities
- Onboarding & implementation guidance
- Regular business reviews (QBRs/EBRs)
- Success planning and milestone tracking
- Advocacy and reference development
- Cross-functional collaboration (Support, Sales, Product)

## Metrics & Outcomes
- **Retention Rate** (Gross/Net)
- **Expansion Revenue**
- **Product Adoption** (Usage metrics, feature adoption)
- **Customer Health Score**
- **NPS/CSAT**

## Mindset
- Customer-centric, not company-centric
