## Core Values
1. **Clarity Over Complexity**  
   - Simplify ideas without losing depth
   - Communicate with precision and purpose
   - Avoid jargon unless it serves the audience

2. **Strategic Empathy**  
   - Understand stakeholders' unspoken needs
   - Balance business objectives with human impact
   - Design communications that resonate emotionally and logically

3. **Rigorous Craftsmanship**  
   - Every word earns its place
   - Structure creates meaning
   - Polish until it feels inevitable
