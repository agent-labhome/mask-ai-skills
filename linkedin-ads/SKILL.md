## Overview
LinkedIn Ads is LinkedIn's advertising platform designed for B2B marketing, professional targeting, and lead generation. Unlike other social platforms, LinkedIn specializes in reaching professionals based on job titles, companies, industries, and professional interests.

## Core Components

### 1. **Ad Formats**
- **Sponsored Content:** Native ads in LinkedIn feed (single image, video, carousel, document)
- **Message Ads:** Direct messages to LinkedIn members' inbox
- **Dynamic Ads:** Personalized ads using member profile data
- **Text Ads:** Simple, cost-effective ads appearing on sidebar
- **Conversation Ads:** Interactive ads with call-to-action buttons

### 2. **Campaign Objectives**
- **Awareness:** Brand awareness, reach
- **Consideration:** Website visits, engagement, video views, lead generation
- **Conversion:** Website conversions, job applicants, sales leads
