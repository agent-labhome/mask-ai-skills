# LinkedIn Ads Skill Guide

## Overview
LinkedIn Ads is the premier B2B advertising platform for reaching professionals, decision-makers, and industry-specific audiences. Unlike other social platforms, LinkedIn focuses on professional context, making it ideal for lead generation, brand awareness in business contexts, and recruitment marketing.

## Core Concepts

### 1. **LinkedIn Ad Formats**
- **Sponsored Content**: Native ads that appear in the LinkedIn feed (single image, video, carousel, document)
- **Message Ads**: Direct messages to LinkedIn members' inboxes
- **Dynamic Ads**: Personalized ads using member profile data
- **Text Ads**: Simple, cost-effective ads appearing on sidebar
