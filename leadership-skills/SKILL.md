# Leadership Skills

**Core Principles:**
- **Vision:** Set clear direction and inspire others.
- **Integrity:** Lead by example with honesty and consistency.
- **Empowerment:** Delegate effectively and trust your team.
- **Communication:** Listen actively and convey ideas clearly.
- **Accountability:** Take ownership of outcomes, both successes and failures.

**Key Practices:**
- Provide constructive feedback.
- Make timely, informed decisions.
- Resolve conflicts constructively.
- Adapt to change and navigate uncertainty.
- Recognize and develop talent in others.
