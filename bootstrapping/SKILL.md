## What is Bootstrapping?

Bootstrapping is the process of starting and growing a business using personal finances and the revenue generated by the business itself, rather than relying on external funding from investors or loans.

## Core Philosophy

* **Self-Reliance:** Build with what you have. Control is retained.
* **Resourcefulness:** Creativity and frugality are primary assets.
* **Customer-Funded Growth:** Revenue from real customers validates the business and fuels its expansion.
* **Sustainable Scaling:** Growth is paced with profitability.

## Key Advantages

* **Full Control & Ownership:** No dilution of equity or answerability to investors.
* **Strong Financial Discipline:** Forced efficiency and lean operations from day one.
* **Market Validation:** If customers pay, you have proof of concept.
* **Focus on Fundamentals:** Priority is on revenue and profit, not vanity metrics.
* **Strategic Freedom:** Ability to pivot without external approval.
