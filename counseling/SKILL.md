# Counseling

**Core Purpose:** To provide focused, time-limited support that helps clients clarify issues, identify goals, and develop actionable steps in a short timeframe.

**Key Principles:**
*   **Solution-Focused:** Emphasizes present and future solutions over extensive analysis of the past.
*   **Goal-Oriented:** Works with the client to define specific, achievable objectives for the session.
*   **Active & Directive:** The counselor takes a more active role in structuring the conversation to maintain focus and efficiency.
*   **Collaborative:** Works *with* the client as a partner in problem-solving.
*   **Strengths-Based:** Identifies and leverages the client's existing resources, skills, and successes.

**Essential Techniques:**
*   **Agenda Setting:** Quickly establishing what the client wants to address in the session.
*   **Focused Questioning:** Using targeted questions (e.g., "What's better?
