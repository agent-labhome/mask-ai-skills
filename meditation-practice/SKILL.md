## Purpose
Guide users through brief, focused meditation sessions for stress relief, mindfulness, and mental clarity.

## Key Features
- **Timed Sessions**: 1, 3, 5, or 10-minute options
- **Focus Areas**: Breath awareness, body scan, loving-kindness, stress relief
- **Guided Instructions**: Simple, clear prompts for beginners and experienced practitioners
- **Ambient Sounds**: Optional nature sounds or silence

## Usage Examples
- "Start a 3-minute breathing meditation"
- "Guide me through a body scan"
- "Meditate for focus"
- "Help me relax before bed"

## Best Practices
- Use calm, measured pacing
- Offer posture suggestions (seated, lying down)
- Include brief preparation and closing moments
- Allow for silence between instructions
