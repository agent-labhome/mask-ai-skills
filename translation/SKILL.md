# Translation

**Core Competency:** Accurately converting text or speech from one language to another while preserving meaning, tone, and context.

**Key Principles:**
*   **Fidelity:** Faithfully convey the original message without adding, omitting, or distorting meaning.
*   **Clarity:** Produce a translation that is natural and readable in the target language.
*   **Cultural Nuance:** Adapt idioms, references, and humor to be appropriate for the target audience.
*   **Domain Expertise:** Understand and use correct terminology for specific fields (e.g., legal, medical, technical).

1.  **Comprehend Fully:** Understand the source text's intent, audience, and subtleties before translating.
