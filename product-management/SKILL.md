## Overview
Product Management is the discipline of guiding a product through its lifecycle—from conception to launch, growth, and eventual retirement. Product Managers (PMs) act as the \"CEO of the product,\" balancing business, technology, and user needs to deliver value.

## Core Competencies

### 1. **Strategic Thinking & Vision**
- **What it is:** Defining the long-term direction and \"why\" behind a product
- **Practical Application:**
  - Create a product vision statement (1-2 sentences describing the future you're building)
  - Develop a product strategy document aligning with company goals
  - Conduct SWOT analysis (Strengths, Weaknesses, Opportunities, Threats)
  - Use the \"Jobs to Be Done\" framework to understand customer needs

### 2. **Market & User Research**
- **What it is:** Understanding customer problems and market opportunities
- **Practical Application:**
  - Conduct user interviews (prepare script, ask open-ended questions, listen actively)
  - Analyze competitor products using feature comparison matrices
  - Create user personas (fictional representations of key user segments)
  - Map user journeys to identify pain points and opportunities
  - Use tools: SurveyMonkey, UserTesting, Hotjar, Google Analytics
