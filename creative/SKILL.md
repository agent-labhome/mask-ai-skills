## Purpose
A structured framework for developing clear, actionable creative briefs that align stakeholders, define objectives, and guide creative execution.

## Core Components
1. **Project Overview** - Background, context, and business need
2. **Objectives** - Measurable goals and success criteria
3. **Target Audience** - Primary and secondary audiences with key insights
4. **Key Message** - Core communication and value proposition
5. **Tone & Voice** - Desired brand personality and emotional resonance
6. **Deliverables** - Specific outputs and formats required
7. **Timeline & Budget** - Practical constraints and milestones
8. **Stakeholders** - Decision-makers and approval chain
