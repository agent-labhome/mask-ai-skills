## Core Competencies

### Foundational Proficiency
- **Arithmetic**: Mastery of operations, fractions, decimals, percentages
- **Algebra**: Solving equations, manipulating expressions, understanding functions
- **Geometry**: Spatial reasoning, properties of shapes, measurement
- **Statistics**: Data interpretation, probability, basic inference

### Applied Thinking
- **Quantitative Reasoning**: Translating real-world problems into mathematical terms
- **Logical Deduction**: Following step-by-step proofs and arguments
- **Pattern Recognition**: Identifying sequences, relationships, and structures
- **Modeling**: Creating simplified mathematical representations of complex situations

### Problem-Solving Framework
1. **Understand**: Parse the problem, identify known/unknown variables
2. **Plan**: Select appropriate methods and tools
3. **Execute**: Perform calculations accurately
4. **Verify**: Check reasonableness, test special cases, confirm units
