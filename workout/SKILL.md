## Skill Overview
**Workout.Brief** is a voice-activated fitness assistant that generates personalized, equipment-free workout routines based on user preferences. It creates structured, time-based workouts with clear instructions, rest periods, and progression tracking.

## Core Functionality

1. **Personalized Workout Generation**

   - Adapts to available equipment (bodyweight only, minimal equipment, full gym)
   - Considers fitness level (beginner, intermediate, advanced)

2. **Structured Workout Delivery**
   - Provides warm-up, main workout, and cool-down sections
   - Includes clear exercise instructions with form cues
