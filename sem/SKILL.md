# SEM Brief - SKILL Documentation

## Overview
SEM Brief is a structured framework for creating and analyzing SEM (Scanning Electron Microscope) experimental briefs. This skill helps researchers systematically document SEM parameters, sample information, and experimental objectives to ensure reproducibility and clarity in materials characterization.

## Core Components

### 1. **Sample Information**
- **Sample ID/Name**: Unique identifier for the sample
- **Material Composition**: Chemical formula, alloy composition, or material type
- **Sample Preparation**: Coating (Au, Pt, C, etc.), mounting method, cleaning procedure
- **Sample Dimensions**: Size, thickness, and geometry
- **Special Considerations**: Beam-sensitive materials, charging issues, contamination risks

### 2. **Experimental Objectives**
- **Primary Goal**: What information needs to be obtained?
