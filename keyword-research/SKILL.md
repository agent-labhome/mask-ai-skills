## What is Keyword Research?

Keyword research is the process of discovering and analyzing the search terms people enter into search engines to inform content strategy, SEO efforts, and business decisions. It's the foundation of understanding user intent and connecting your content with audience needs.

## Core Principles

### 1. Search Intent
Every keyword represents a user's intent, typically categorized as:
- **Informational**: Seeking knowledge (e.g., \"how to bake sourdough\")
- **Commercial**: Researching before purchase (e.g., \"best DSLR cameras 2024\")
- **Transactional**: Ready to buy (e.g., \"buy Nikon Z5 camera\")
- **Navigational**: Looking for specific site/page (e.g., \"Facebook login\")

## Practical Guide: The Keyword Research Process

### Phase 1: Foundation (1-2 hours)

**Step 1: Define Your Goals**
- What do you want to achieve? (Traffic, conversions, brand awareness)
- Who is your target audience?
- What problems do you solve?

**Step 2: Brainstorm Seed Keywords**
- List core topics related to your business
- Include product names, services, industry terms
- Think like your customer: What would they search for?

*Example for a yoga studio:*
- yoga for beginners
- prenatal yoga
- yoga near me
- morning yoga routine
