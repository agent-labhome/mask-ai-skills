## Core Competencies

### 1. **Financial Record-Keeping**
- **Double-Entry Accounting**: Maintain accurate general ledgers using debit/credit principles
- **Transaction Recording**: Document all financial transactions (sales, purchases, payments, receipts)
- **Source Document Management**: Organize and file invoices, receipts, bank statements, bills
- **Chart of Accounts**: Maintain and update account structure for proper categorization

### 2. **Accounts Management**
- **Accounts Receivable**: Track customer invoices, payments, and aging reports
- **Accounts Payable**: Manage vendor bills, payment scheduling, and expense tracking
- **Bank Reconciliation**: Match bank statements with ledger entries monthly
- **Petty Cash Management**: Track and reconcile small cash transactions

### 3. **Financial Reporting**
- **Trial Balance**: Prepare and verify ledger accuracy before financial statements
- **Basic Financial Statements**: Generate income statements and balance sheets
