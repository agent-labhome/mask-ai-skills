## What is Personal Branding?
Personal branding is the intentional, strategic practice of defining and communicating your unique value, expertise, and character to a target audience. It’s not about self-promotion but about **consistent self-presentation** that builds trust, differentiates you, and creates professional opportunities.

---

## Core Principles
1. **Authenticity:** Your brand must align with your true values, strengths, and passions.
2. **Consistency:** Unified messaging across platforms and interactions.
3. **Value-Centric:** Focus on what you offer others, not just what you want.
4. **Visibility:** Strategic presence where your audience engages.
5. **Evolution:** Your brand should adapt as you grow professionally.
