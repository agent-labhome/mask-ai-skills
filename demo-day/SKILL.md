## Overview
Demo Day is the ability to effectively present a project, product, or idea through a live demonstration to an audience of stakeholders, investors, peers, or judges. This skill combines technical knowledge with storytelling and public speaking to create an engaging, memorable showcase.

## Core Competencies

### 1. Demo Preparation
- **Technical Setup**: Ensure all equipment, software, and backups are ready
- **Script Development**: Create a clear narrative arc (problem → solution → impact)
- **Time Management**: Structure presentation within strict time limits (typically 3-10 minutes)
- **Contingency Planning**: Prepare for technical failures with backup plans

### 2. Storytelling & Narrative
- **Hook Creation**: Start with compelling problem statement or surprising insight
- **Audience Alignment**: Tailor message to audience knowledge level and interests
- **Emotional Connection**: Incorporate user stories or real-world impact
