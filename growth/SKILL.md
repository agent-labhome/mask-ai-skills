## Purpose
A Growth Brief is a strategic document that aligns cross-functional teams around a focused growth initiative. It defines the problem, opportunity, hypothesis, and execution plan for a specific growth experiment or campaign.

## Core Components

### 1. **Problem Statement**
- Clear description of the current challenge or opportunity
- Data-backed context about why this matters now
- User pain points or business gaps being addressed

### 2. **Goal & Success Metrics**
- **Primary Metric:** The single most important success indicator
- **Secondary Metrics:** Supporting indicators of health/quality
- **Target:** Specific, measurable target with timeframe
- **Baseline:** Current performance for comparison

### 3. **Target Audience**
- Specific segment(s) being addressed
- User behaviors, needs, or characteristics
- Size of the opportunity (total addressable market)
