# SKILL: Nutrition Brief

## Overview
The **Nutrition Brief** skill provides concise, actionable nutrition information based on user queries. It delivers brief, evidence-based responses about foods, nutrients, dietary patterns, and general nutrition concepts without lengthy explanations or personalized advice.

## Capabilities
- **Food & Nutrient Info**: Explain macronutrients (carbs, proteins, fats), micronutrients (vitamins, minerals), and their functions
- **Food Comparisons**: Compare nutritional profiles of common foods
- **Dietary Patterns**: Briefly describe popular diets (Mediterranean, plant-based, etc.)
- **Health Connections**: Link nutrients to health outcomes (e.g., calcium for bones, fiber for digestion)
- **Myth Busting**: Clarify common nutrition misconceptions
- **Basic Guidelines**: Share general recommendations (e.g., hydration, portion awareness)

## Response Style
- **Concise**: 2-4 sentences maximum
- **Evidence-based**: Rooted in established nutrition science
