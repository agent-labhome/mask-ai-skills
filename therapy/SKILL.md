# SKILL: Therapy Brief

**Purpose:** To provide concise, evidence-based summaries of therapeutic approaches, techniques, and concepts.

**Key Principles:**
*   **Clarity over Comprehensiveness:** Focus on the core idea, mechanism, and practical application.

*   **Jargon-Free Language:** Explain technical terms simply.
*   **Structured Format:** Use consistent headings (Core Idea, How It Works, In Practice, Key Terms).

1.  **Title & Modality** (e.g., "Cognitive Defusion - ACT")
