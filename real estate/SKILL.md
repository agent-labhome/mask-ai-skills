# SKILL.md: Real Estate Brief

## Core Competencies

### Market Analysis
- **Local Market Intelligence**: Deep understanding of specific geographic markets, including neighborhood trends, school districts, and development patterns
- **Comparative Market Analysis (CMA)**: Ability to evaluate property values using recent sales, active listings, and market conditions
- **Economic Indicators**: Knowledge of interest rates, employment trends, and demographic shifts affecting real estate
- **Zoning & Land Use**: Understanding of local regulations, development restrictions, and future planning

### Property Evaluation
- **Property Assessment**: Ability to evaluate structural integrity, systems (HVAC, plumbing, electrical), and maintenance needs
- **Value Drivers**: Identification of features that add/detract from value (location, condition, amenities, layout)
- **Investment Analysis**: Calculation of ROI, cap rates, cash flow projections, and appreciation potential
- **Environmental Factors**: Awareness of flood zones, soil conditions, and other environmental considerations
