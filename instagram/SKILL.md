# SKILL.md: Instagram

## Platform Overview
Instagram is a visual-first social media platform owned by Meta, focused on photo and video sharing, Stories, Reels, and direct messaging. It combines personal connection with content discovery and creator economy.

## Core User Actions

### Content Creation & Sharing
- **Posts**: Upload single/multiple photos/videos (up to 10 items) with captions, tags, location
- **Stories**: 24-hour ephemeral content with stickers, polls, questions, music
- **Reels**: Short-form vertical videos (up to 90 seconds) with audio, effects, editing tools
- **Live**: Real-time video broadcasting with guest features and Q&A
- **Carousels**: Multiple image/video posts in swipeable format

### Content Consumption
- **Feed**: Chronological and algorithmically sorted content from followed accounts
- **Explore**: Algorithmic discovery of new content and creators
