## Overview
A **Design Brief** is a strategic document that outlines the objectives, scope, and requirements for a design project. It serves as a foundational agreement between stakeholders and designers, ensuring alignment and clarity from the outset. This skill involves creating, interpreting, and utilizing design briefs to guide successful project outcomes.

## Core Components of a Design Brief

### 1. **Project Overview**
- **Project Name:** Clear identifier for the project.
- **Client/Company:** Who is requesting the design?
- **Date:** When the brief was created/approved.

### 2. **Objectives & Goals**
- **Primary Goal:** The main purpose of the design (e.g., increase brand awareness, improve user experience, launch a new product).
