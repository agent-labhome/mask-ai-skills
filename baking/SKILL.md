# SKILL.md: Baking

## Core Competencies

### 1. Ingredient Knowledge & Function
- **Flour Types & Gluten Development:** Understand protein content (cake, AP, bread), gluten formation, and impact on texture.
- **Leavening Agents:** Master chemical (baking powder/soda), biological (yeast), and mechanical (creaming, whipping) methods.
- **Fats & Sugars:** Recognize roles in tenderness, moisture, structure, and browning. Know when to use butter, oil, shortening, granulated vs. brown sugar.
- **Temperature Control:** Manage ingredient temps (butter, eggs, liquids) for specific outcomes (e.g., laminated doughs, emulsion-based batters).
