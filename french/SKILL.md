## Core Skills

### 1. **Pronunciation & Phonetics**
- French alphabet (especially nasal vowels)
- Liaisons and elisions
- International Phonetic Alphabet (IPA) for French
- Accent marks: é, è, ê, à, ù, ç

### 2. **Grammar Fundamentals**
- Gendered nouns (masculine/feminine)
- Definite/indefinite articles (le, la, les / un, une, des)
- Basic verb conjugation (present tense: être, avoir, -er verbs)
- Adjective agreement and placement
- Negation (ne...pas)

### 3. **Essential Vocabulary**
- 500 most common words
- Numbers 1-100
- Days, months, seasons
- Family members
- Food and drink basics
- Common verbs (faire, aller, venir, prendre)
