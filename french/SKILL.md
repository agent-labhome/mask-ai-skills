## Core Structure
- **Alphabet**: 26 letters (same as English) + diacritics (é, è, ê, à, ù, ç)
- **Pronunciation**: Nasal vowels, silent consonants, liaison rules
- **Grammar**: Gendered nouns (masculine/feminine), verb conjugations, formal/informal address

## Key Learning Areas

### 1. Essentials
- Greetings and introductions
- Numbers 1-100
- Days, months, seasons
- Basic questions (who, what, where, when, why)

### 2. Grammar Fundamentals
- Present tense conjugation (-er, -ir, -re verbs)
- Articles (le, la, les, un, une, des)
- Subject pronouns
- Negation (ne...pas)

### 3. Practical Vocabulary
- Food and dining
- Travel and directions
- Family and relationships
