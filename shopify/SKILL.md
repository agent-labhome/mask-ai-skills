# SKILL.md: Shopify

## Overview
**Shopify** is a leading commerce platform that enables businesses to create online stores, manage products, process payments, and handle shipping. This skill focuses on understanding its core capabilities, ecosystem, and strategic use cases for businesses and developers.

---

## Core Competencies

### 1. Platform Fundamentals
*   **Storefront & Admin:** Understand the merchant-facing Admin panel and customer-facing Storefront.
*   **Plans & Pricing:** Know the differences between core plans (Basic, Shopify, Advanced) and Shopify Plus.
*   **Key Concepts:** Products, Collections, Customers, Orders, Discounts, Themes.

### 2. Store Setup & Management
*   **Domain & Branding:** Configure custom domains, logos, and basic brand settings.
