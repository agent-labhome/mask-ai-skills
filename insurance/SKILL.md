## Overview
The **Insurance Brief** skill enables users to generate concise, structured summaries of insurance policies, claims, or coverage scenarios. It distills complex insurance documents and situations into clear, actionable briefs for quick understanding and decision-making.

## Core Capabilities

### 1. **Policy Summarization**
- Extracts key elements from insurance policies
- Identifies coverage types, limits, deductibles, and exclusions
- Highlights premium information and payment terms
- Flags important conditions and requirements

### 2. **Claims Analysis**
- Summarizes claim circumstances and status
- Identifies covered vs. non-covered items
- Calculates potential payouts based on policy terms
- Notes documentation requirements and deadlines

### 3. **Coverage Assessment**
- Evaluates what specific scenarios or items are covered
- Compares different policy options or riders
- Identifies coverage gaps or overlaps
- Provides risk assessment based on coverage levels
