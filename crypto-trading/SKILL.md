# SKILL: Crypto Trading

## Metadata
- **Domain**: Finance & Technology
- **Complexity**: Intermediate to Advanced
- **Time to Basic Proficiency**: 6-12 months
- **Key Prerequisites**: Financial literacy, risk management mindset, basic technical analysis
- **Tools**: Trading platforms, charting software, portfolio trackers, news aggregators
- **Risk Level**: High (capital loss possible)
- **Regulatory Status**: Varies by jurisdiction (often unregulated or lightly regulated)

## Overview
Crypto trading involves buying and selling cryptocurrencies on various exchanges to profit from price movements. Unlike long-term investing (\"HODLing\"), trading focuses on shorter timeframes and technical market dynamics.

## Core Competencies

### 1. Market Understanding
- **Blockchain Fundamentals**: Understanding what gives different cryptocurrencies value
- **Market Cycles**: Recognizing bull/bear markets and seasonal patterns
- **Correlations**: How crypto markets relate to traditional markets (stocks, commodities)
- **Sector Analysis**: Differences between DeFi, NFTs, Layer 1s, Layer 2s, memecoins

### 2. Technical Analysis (TA)
- **Chart Patterns**: Support/resistance, triangles, head & shoulders, flags
- **Indicators**: 
  - Trend: Moving averages (MA, EMA), MACD
  - Momentum: RSI, Stochastic
  - Volatility: Bollinger Bands, ATR
  - Volume: OBV, Volume profile
- **Candlestick Patterns**: Doji, hammer, engulfing patterns, morning/evening stars
- **Multi-timeframe Analysis**: Using higher timeframes for direction, lower for entries
