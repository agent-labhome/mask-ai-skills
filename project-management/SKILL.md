## Overview
Project Management is the discipline of planning, organizing, securing, and managing resources to achieve specific goals within defined constraints. It involves balancing scope, time, cost, quality, and stakeholder expectations to deliver successful outcomes.

## Core Competencies

### 1. **Project Lifecycle Management**
- **Initiation:** Defining project purpose, scope, and stakeholders
- **Planning:** Developing detailed project plans, schedules, and budgets
- **Execution:** Coordinating people and resources to implement plans
- **Monitoring & Controlling:** Tracking progress and making adjustments
- **Closing:** Formal acceptance and documentation of lessons learned

### 2. **Methodologies & Frameworks**
- **Waterfall:** Sequential, phase-based approach
- **Agile/Scrum:** Iterative, flexible methodology
- **Kanban:** Visual workflow management
- **Hybrid:** Combining elements from multiple methodologies
- **Critical Path Method (CPM):** Identifying essential tasks

- Risk Register
- Status Reports
