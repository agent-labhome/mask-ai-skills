## Core Competencies

### Technical Proficiency
- **Platform Navigation**: Mastery of learning management systems (LMS) like Canvas, Moodle, Blackboard, Coursera, edX
- **Digital Literacy**: Comfort with video conferencing (Zoom, Teams), discussion forums, and cloud-based collaboration tools
- **Self-Directed Learning**: Ability to identify learning needs, set goals, and independently pursue knowledge
- **Information Management**: Organizing digital materials, bookmarking resources, and maintaining digital notes

### Learning Strategies
- **Time Management**: Creating and adhering to study schedules, prioritizing tasks, avoiding procrastination
- **Active Engagement**: Participating in discussions, asking questions, and applying concepts to real-world scenarios
- **Resource Utilization**: Effectively using recorded lectures, supplementary materials, and online libraries
- **Assessment Preparation**: Adapting study methods for online quizzes, exams, and project submissions
