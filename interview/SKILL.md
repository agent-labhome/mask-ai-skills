# SKILL.md for Interview Briefs

## Core Competencies

### 1. Information Synthesis & Analysis
- **Pattern Recognition**: Identify recurring themes, requirements, and constraints across multiple sources
- **Critical Filtering**: Distinguish essential requirements from background noise or secondary information
- **Contextual Understanding**: Connect brief details to industry standards, role expectations, and company context
- **Gap Identification**: Spot missing information or ambiguous requirements that need clarification

### 2. Brief Deconstruction
- **Hierarchical Structuring**: Organize information into primary objectives, secondary goals, and supporting details
- **Requirement Categorization**: Classify requirements as:
  - Must-haves (non-negotiable)
  - Should-haves (important but flexible)
  - Nice-to-haves (bonus qualifications)
- **Stakeholder Analysis**: Identify explicit and implicit stakeholder needs from the brief
