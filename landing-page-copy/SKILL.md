## 1. Core Principles
- **Clarity over cleverness:** Communicate value instantly.
- **Benefit-driven:** Focus on what the user gains, not just features.
- **Audience-first:** Tailor voice, pain points, and aspirations to the target user.
- **Goal-oriented:** Every element should guide toward a single primary action (CTA).

## 2. Structural Framework (AIDA)
- **Attention:** Compelling hero headline & sub-headline.
- **Interest:** Expand on key benefits and address core desires.
- **Desire:** Build credibility with social proof (testimonials, logos, data).
- **Action:** Clear, persuasive Call-to-Action with a sense of urgency or value.
