## Core Competency
Structuring ambiguous problems into actionable consulting engagements through strategic questioning, scope definition, and deliverable alignment.

## Key Components

### 1. Problem Definition
- **Root Cause Analysis**: Distinguish symptoms from underlying issues
- **Stakeholder Mapping**: Identify decision-makers, influencers, and affected parties
- **Success Criteria**: Define what \"solved\" looks like (qualitative & quantitative)

### 2. Scope Crafting
- **Boundary Setting**: What's in scope vs. out of scope
- **Phase Planning**: Logical sequencing of work packages
- **Resource Alignment**: Client vs. consultant responsibilities

### 3. Deliverable Design
- **Output Specification**: Reports, models, workshops, implementations
- **Decision Support**: How deliverables enable client action
- **Knowledge Transfer**: Ensuring client capability post-engagement
