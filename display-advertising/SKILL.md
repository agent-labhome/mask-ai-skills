## Core Competency
Ability to create, interpret, and execute effective display advertising briefs that align business objectives with creative execution across digital display environments.

## Skill Components

### 1. **Strategic Foundation**
- Translate business goals into measurable advertising objectives
- Identify and define target audiences with precision
- Understand customer journey mapping in display contexts
- Set appropriate KPIs (CTR, viewability, conversions, brand lift)

### 2. **Technical Specifications**
- Format knowledge: banners, rich media, native, video, interactive
- Ad dimensions and specifications (IAB standards)
- File size limitations and optimization techniques
- Tracking and measurement requirements
- Platform-specific requirements (Google Display Network, programmatic, social)

### 3. **Creative Requirements**
- Brand guidelines integration
- Messaging hierarchy and value proposition clarity
- Visual asset specifications (images, logos, typography)
- Copywriting constraints and best practices
