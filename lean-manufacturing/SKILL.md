## Overview
Lean Manufacturing is a systematic approach to identifying and eliminating waste (non-value-added activities) through continuous improvement while delivering value to customers. Originating from the Toyota Production System, Lean focuses on creating more value with fewer resources by optimizing flow and eliminating muda (waste), mura (unevenness), and muri (overburden).
