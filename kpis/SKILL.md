# SKILL: KPIs (Key Performance Indicators)

## Core Competency
Defining, tracking, and analyzing measurable metrics that align with business objectives.

## Key Abilities
- **Definition:** Selecting relevant metrics that reflect strategic goals
- **Tracking:** Monitoring KPI performance over time
- **Analysis:** Interpreting data to inform decisions
- **Communication:** Presenting KPI insights clearly to stakeholders

## Quick Reference
- **SMART KPIs:** Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, Time-bound
- **Leading vs. Lagging:** Predictive indicators vs. outcome measurements
- **Dashboard Basics:** Visualize key metrics for quick comprehension
- **Action Orientation:** Connect KPI movement to concrete next steps

## Common Applications
- Performance reporting
- Goal setting and alignment
- Strategic planning
- Resource allocation decisions
