## What is Voice Search SEO?
Voice Search SEO is the practice of optimizing digital content to appear in voice search results from virtual assistants like Siri, Google Assistant, Alexa, and Cortana. Unlike traditional text-based searches, voice queries are typically longer, conversational, and question-based, requiring specialized optimization strategies.
