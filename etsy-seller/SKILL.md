# Etsy Seller SKILL.md

## Core Skills
- **Product Photography:** Clean, well-lit images with consistent styling.
- **SEO Optimization:** Strategic use of keywords in titles, tags, and descriptions.
- **Customer Communication:** Prompt, professional, and empathetic messaging.
- **Listing Crafting:** Clear descriptions, accurate policies, and compelling storytelling.

## Key Tools
- Etsy Seller App
- Canva or similar for graphics
- Shipping platform (Etsy Labels, Pirate Ship)
- Inventory tracking system (spreadsheet or app)

## Best Practices
- **Packaging:** Unboxing experience matters. Use branded touches.
- **Reviews:** Politely encourage feedback; respond professionally to all reviews.
- **Renewals:** Manually renew listings to maintain search ranking vs. auto-renew.
- **Analytics:** Regularly check Shop Stats to guide decisions.
