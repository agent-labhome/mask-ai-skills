## Overview
Renewal Management is the systematic process of managing customer contract renewals to maximize retention, minimize churn, and identify expansion opportunities. This skill combines proactive customer engagement, data analysis, and cross-functional coordination to ensure customers renew their subscriptions or contracts successfully.
