# SKILL.md: Restaurant

## **Skill Name**
Restaurant

## **Brief**
A skill for finding restaurants, checking availability, and making reservations.

## **Description**
The Restaurant skill helps users discover dining options, check real-time availability, and book tables. It can filter by cuisine, location, price range, dietary restrictions, and occasion. The skill integrates with reservation platforms and restaurant databases to provide accurate, up-to-date information and confirm bookings.

## **Use Cases**
1.  **Finding a Restaurant:** "Find Italian restaurants near me open now."
2.  **Checking Details:** "What's the menu and price range for [Restaurant Name]?"
3.  **Making a Reservation:** "Book a table for two at [Restaurant Name] for 7 PM tomorrow."
