## Overview

Sales Strategy is the systematic approach to identifying, engaging, and converting prospects into customers while maximizing revenue and customer lifetime value. This skill combines analytical thinking, psychological insight, and tactical execution to create repeatable, scalable sales processes.

## Core Components

### 1. **Market & Customer Analysis**
- **ICP Development:** Define Ideal Customer Profile (demographics, firmographics, psychographics)
- **Market Segmentation:** Divide target market into approachable groups
- **Competitive Analysis:** Identify competitor strengths, weaknesses, and positioning gaps
- **Buyer Persona Creation:** Develop detailed profiles of decision-makers and influencers

### 2. **Sales Process Design**
- **Pipeline Architecture:** Stage definitions and progression criteria
- **Lead Qualification Framework:** BANT, MEDDIC, CHAMP, or custom methodology
- **Sales Cycle Mapping:** Timeline expectations and milestone tracking
- **Handoff Protocols:** Marketing-to-sales and sales-to-customer success transitions

### 3. **Value Proposition & Messaging**

### 4. **Channel & Methodology Selection**

- **Inbound Strategy:** Lead nurturing, content-driven conversion
- **Partnership Strategy:** Channel sales, referral programs, alliances
- **Sales Methodology Alignment:** SPIN, Challenger, Solution Selling, or SNAP
