## Overview
The Partnership skill guides you through creating, structuring, and managing successful business partnerships. This comprehensive framework helps formalize collaborations while protecting all parties' interests and maximizing mutual value.

## When to Use This Skill
- Forming a new business partnership or joint venture

- Expanding through strategic alliances

- Creating channel partnerships or reseller agreements
