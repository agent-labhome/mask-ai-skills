## Overview
LinkedIn Marketing is the strategic use of LinkedIn's platform to achieve business objectives including brand awareness, lead generation, talent acquisition, and industry authority building. Unlike other social platforms, LinkedIn focuses on professional networking and B2B relationships.

## Core Competencies

### 1. Profile Optimization
**Practical Application:**
- **Personal Profiles:** Use professional headshots, keyword-rich headlines (120 characters), and detailed experience sections with measurable achievements
- **Company Pages:** Complete all sections including banner image, description, specialties, and showcase pages for different business units
- **Actionable Tip:** Use LinkedIn's \"Open to Work\" or \"Hiring\" features strategically. For company pages, enable all relevant call-to-action buttons (Contact us, Visit website, Learn more)

### 2. Content Strategy
**Practical Framework:**
- **Content Mix:** Follow the 4-1-1 rule (4 industry insights, 1 soft promotion, 1 direct promotion)
- **Post Types:** Articles (long-form), native video (3x more engagement), carousels, documents (PDF presentations), polls, and text posts
- **Best Times to Post:** Tuesday-Thursday, 8-10 AM or 12-2 PM in your target audience's timezone
- **Practical Tool:** Use LinkedIn's Content Suggestions feature to discover trending topics in your industry
