## Overview
Guest posting is the practice of writing and publishing content on another website or blog to reach a new audience, build authority, and earn backlinks. It's a core content marketing and SEO strategy.

- **Improves SEO:** Earns high-quality backlinks to boost domain authority and search rankings.
- **Drives Targeted Traffic:** Attracts relevant visitors likely to convert.
