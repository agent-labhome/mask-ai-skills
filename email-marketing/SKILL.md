# Email Marketing Skill

## Overview
Email marketing is the practice of sending commercial messages, typically to a group of people, using email. It involves building relationships with prospects and customers, driving engagement, and generating conversions through targeted, personalized email communications.

## Core Competencies

### 1. Strategy & Planning
- **List Segmentation**: Dividing email lists into targeted groups based on demographics, behavior, or engagement
- **Campaign Planning**: Developing email calendars and sequences aligned with business goals
- **Goal Setting**: Defining clear KPIs (open rates, click-through rates, conversions)
- **Customer Journey Mapping**: Creating email flows for different stages of the customer lifecycle
