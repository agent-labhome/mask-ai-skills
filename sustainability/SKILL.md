Sustainability is no longer an optional corporate social responsibility initiative but a core business imperative that drives competitive advantage, risk mitigation, and long-term value creation. This guide provides a comprehensive framework for integrating sustainability into business operations, strategy, and culture.

### 1.1 Business Case for Sustainability

- **Market Advantage:** 66% of consumers willing to pay more for sustainable brands (Nielsen)
- **Talent Attraction:** 75% of millennials consider sustainability in employment decisions (Deloitte)

Conduct a double materiality assessment to identify:
- **Financial Materiality:** Sustainability issues that affect financial performance
- **Impact Materiality:** Business activities that significantly impact environment/society
- **Stakeholder Mapping:** Prioritize issues based on stakeholder importance and influence

