## Core Competency
Creating clear, actionable procurement briefs that align stakeholder requirements with organizational procurement strategy.

## Skill Components

### 1. **Requirements Analysis**
- Conduct stakeholder interviews to identify business needs
- Translate operational requirements into procurement specifications
- Distinguish between mandatory requirements and desirable features
- Identify technical, functional, and compliance specifications

### 2. **Market Research**
- Analyze supplier landscape and market capabilities
- Research industry standards and best practices
- Identify potential suppliers and solution providers
- Assess market capacity and availability

### 3. **Document Structure**
- Executive summary and business case
- Detailed technical specifications
- Commercial requirements and terms
- Evaluation criteria and scoring methodology
- Timeline and delivery requirements
