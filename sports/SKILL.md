# SKILL: Sports Brief

## Overview
The **Sports Brief** skill provides concise, up-to-date summaries of recent sports news, scores, and key events. It's designed for users who want a quick, efficient overview of the sports world without diving into lengthy articles.

## Core Functionality

### 1. **News Summaries**
- Delivers brief, digestible summaries of top sports stories.
- Covers major leagues (NFL, NBA, MLB, NHL, Premier League, etc.) and global events.
- Can filter by sport, league, team, or athlete upon request.

### 2. **Scores & Standings**
- Provides recent final scores for games/matches.
- Can give current standings or rankings for leagues/tournaments.
- Updates for live games (e.g., "In the 3rd quarter, Team A leads Team B 24-17").
