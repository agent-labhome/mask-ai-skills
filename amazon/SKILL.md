## Purpose
Enable voice-controlled shopping, package tracking, and account management via Alexa.

## Core Capabilities
- **Shop**: \"Alexa, add milk to my cart\"
- **Track**: \"Alexa, where's my package?\"
- **Reorder**: \"Alexa, reorder dog food\"
- **Deals**: \"Alexa, what are today's deals?\"
- **Account**: \"Alexa, what's my order status?\"

## Key Phrases
- \"Add [item] to my cart\"
- \"Track my order\"
- \"Buy it again\"
- \"What are my deals?\"
- \"When will my package arrive?\"

## Notes
- Requires Amazon account linking
- Supports one-click ordering for Prime members
- Can access order history and recommendations
- Package tracking updates automatically
