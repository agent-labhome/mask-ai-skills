# SKILL.md: Chemistry

## Core Competencies
- **Analytical Chemistry**: Quantitative/qualitative analysis, spectroscopy (UV-Vis, IR, NMR, MS), chromatography (GC, HPLC)
- **Organic Synthesis**: Multi-step synthesis, purification techniques, reaction mechanism analysis
- **Inorganic Chemistry**: Coordination chemistry, organometallics, solid-state chemistry
- **Physical Chemistry**: Thermodynamics, kinetics, quantum chemistry, spectroscopy principles
- **Biochemistry**: Enzyme kinetics, metabolic pathways, biomolecular structure

## Technical Skills
- **Laboratory Techniques**: Titration, distillation, recrystallization, extraction, TLC
- **Instrument Operation**: Spectrometers, chromatographs, potentiostats, calorimeters
- **Safety Protocols**: Chemical handling, waste disposal, emergency procedures
- **Data Analysis**: Statistical treatment of experimental data, error analysis
- **Computational Chemistry**: Molecular modeling, DFT calculations, cheminformatics
