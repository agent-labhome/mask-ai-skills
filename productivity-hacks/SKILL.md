# SKILL: Productivity Hacks

**Purpose:** Optimize focus, efficiency, and output in work and personal tasks.

**Core Principles:**
*   **Intentionality:** Work on what matters most.

*   **Friction Reduction:** Make good habits easy, bad habits hard.

*   **Time Blocking:** Assign specific tasks to fixed calendar slots.

*   **Eat the Frog:** Tackle your most important/dreaded task first.
