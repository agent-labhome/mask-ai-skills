# Gumroad Product Launcher

_Complete guide for launching and selling digital products on Gumroad_

## When to Use

Use this skill when you want to:
- Launch a digital product on Gumroad
- Optimize existing Gumroad listings
- Increase sales and conversions
- Set up pricing tiers and bundles
- Create effective product pages
- Build email lists with Gumroad
- Analyze Gumroad analytics

## Pre-Launch Checklist

### Product Preparation
- [ ] Complete product with all assets
- [ ] Preview files ready
- [ ] Terms of use/license defined
- [ ] Support email set up

### Page Optimization
- [ ] Compelling title and description
- [ ] Cover image (1600x900 recommended)
- [ ] Preview images/screenshots
- [ ] Pricing strategy decided
- [ ] Tier pricing if applicable

### Marketing Assets
- [ ] Sales copy written
- [ ] Launch email sequence ready
- [ ] Social proof prepared
- [ ] Launch date scheduled

## Pricing Strategies

### Single Price
- Simple, straightforward
- Best for focused products

### Tiered Pricing
- **Starter** ($): Basic version
- **Pro** ($$): Full version
- **Premium** ($$$): Full + bonuses

### Bundle Deals
- Combine related products
- Percentage discounts for bundles

## Gumroad Features

### Overlay vs Landing Page
- **Overlay**: Non-intrusive, lower conversion
- **Landing Page**: Full control, higher conversion

### Email Collection
- Free + shipping (physical products)
- Lead magnets with follow-up
- Newsletter integration

### Affiliate Program
- Enable for others to promote
- Set commission (default 10%)
- Track affiliate sales

### Analytics Dashboard
- Sales by product
- Traffic sources
- Conversion rates
- Revenue trends

## Launch Strategies

### Pre-Launch (2-4 weeks)
- Build waitlist
- Tease on social media
- Collect emails

### Launch Day
- Announce to email list
- Post on social platforms
- Reach out to influencers
- Product hunt submission

### Post-Launch
- Follow up with buyers
- Request reviews
- Create content about product
- Upsell opportunities

## Conversion Optimization

1. **Compelling Cover** - First impression matters
2. **Clear Description** - Benefits over features
3. **Social Proof** - Testimonials, sales numbers
4. **Risk Reversal** - Money-back guarantee
5. **Scarcity** - Limited time offers

## Tools & Integrations

- **Gumroad API** - Custom integrations
- **Zapier** - Connect to email, Slack
- **ConvertKit** - Email marketing
- **Notion** - Product documentation
- **Google Analytics** - Track traffic
