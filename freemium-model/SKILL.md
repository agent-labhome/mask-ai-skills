## Overview
The freemium model is a business strategy where a product or service is provided free of charge, but money (a premium) is charged for proprietary features, functionality, or virtual goods. This model leverages free offerings as a customer acquisition tool, converting a portion of users to paying customers over time.

## Core Principles

### 1. **Value Asymmetry**
- Free tier must deliver genuine, standalone value
- Premium features should solve specific, high-value problems
- Clear progression path from free to paid

### 2. **Sustainable Economics**
- Free users' costs must be covered by paying customers
- Conversion rates must support overall business viability
- Lifetime value (LTV) of paying customers > customer acquisition cost (CAC)

### 3. **Frictionless Conversion**
- Easy transition from free to paid
- Clear communication of premium benefits
- Multiple touchpoints for upgrade prompts
