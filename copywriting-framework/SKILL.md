## Purpose
Quickly structure effective copywriting briefs using a proven framework.

## Core Framework (AIDA + 4U)

### 1. ATTENTION (Hook)
- **Urgent:** Why act now?
- **Unique:** What's different?
- **Useful:** What problem solves?
- **Ultra-specific:** Clear promise?

### 2. INTEREST (Problem/Pain)
- Target audience's main frustration
- Emotional impact of problem
- Current inadequate solutions

### 3. DESIRE (Solution/Benefit)
- Your product/service as answer

- Social proof/credibility elements

### 4. ACTION (Clear CTA)
- Single, specific next step
