## Purpose
A concise, structured way to capture essential family information for personal records, emergency planning, or sharing with caregivers.

## Structure
Each entry follows this format:

**[Relation]: [Full Name]**
- **Contact:** [Phone/Email]
- **Notes:** [Key details: e.g., "Emergency contact," "Allergies: penicillin," "School: Lincoln Elementary"]

## Rules
1.  **One family member per entry.**
2.  **Start with the relation** (e.g., Spouse, Child, Parent, Sibling).
3.  **Use bold for labels** (Relation, Contact, Notes).
4.  **Keep notes brief and factual.** Focus on critical info.
5.  **List entries in order of immediate household first, then extended family.**
