## Core Competencies

**Beginner (Level 1-2)**
- Water acclimation and breath control
- Basic floating (front/back)
- Elementary propulsion (dog paddle, flutter kick with board)
- Front crawl arm motion with support
- Safe entry/exit from pool

**Intermediate (Level 3-4)**
- Unassisted front crawl with rotary breathing
- Backstroke with proper technique
- Breaststroke kick and timing
- Treading water (1-2 minutes)
- Basic diving from side
- Introduction to flip turns

**Advanced (Level 5-6)**
- Efficient front crawl with bilateral breathing
- Competitive backstroke with shoulder roll
- Full breaststroke coordination
- Butterfly kick and dolphin motion
- Racing starts and flip turns
