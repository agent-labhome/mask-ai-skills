## Core Principle
Influence others' thoughts or actions through reasoned argument, not coercion.

## Key Techniques

**Listen First**
- Understand their perspective before speaking
- Identify underlying needs and objections

**Frame Your Case**
- Align with their values and interests
- Present clear benefits, not just features
- Use \"we\" language to build collaboration

**Structure Matters**
- Start with common ground
- Present evidence clearly
- Address counterpoints proactively
- End with a clear, actionable ask

**Build Credibility**
- Be consistent and reliable
- Show genuine understanding of their position
