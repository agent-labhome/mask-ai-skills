# SKILL: Retention Brief

## Purpose
A Retention Brief is a strategic document that outlines the core elements of a customer retention strategy. It serves as a concise, actionable guide for teams to align on goals, target audiences, key messages, and tactics to reduce churn and increase customer loyalty.

## Core Components

A comprehensive Retention Brief should include:

1.  **Objective:** The primary goal (e.g., reduce churn by X%, increase LTV by Y%, improve NPS to Z).
2.  **Target Audience:** Specific segment(s) of customers at risk or with high potential (e.g., "Power Users showing decreased activity," "Annual subscribers within 30 days of renewal").
3.  **Key Insight/Problem:** The "why" behind the retention effort (e.g., "Users who don't complete onboarding within 7 days have 60% higher churn").
