**SKILL: Communication**

**Core Definition:** The effective exchange of information, ideas, and intent between individuals or groups, ensuring clarity, understanding, and purpose.

**Key Components:**
*   **Clarity:** Concisely articulating your message.
*   **Active Listening:** Fully concentrating, understanding, and responding to the speaker.
*   **Empathy & Tone:** Understanding the audience's perspective and adjusting delivery accordingly.
*   **Context Awareness:** Adapting style (verbal, written, non-verbal) to the situation (e.g., formal report, team chat, difficult conversation).
*   **Feedback Loop:** Confirming understanding and encouraging questions.

**Manifests As:**
*   Writing clear emails/documentation.
*   Leading productive meetings.
*   Giving and receiving constructive feedback.
*   Presenting ideas persuasively.
*   Resolving misunderstandings efficiently.

**Antipatterns:**
*   Assuming instead of confirming.
*   One-way information dumping.
