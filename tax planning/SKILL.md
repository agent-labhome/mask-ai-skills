## Overview
Tax Planning is the strategic analysis and arrangement of financial affairs to minimize tax liability within the legal framework. This skill involves understanding tax laws, regulations, and incentives to make informed financial decisions that optimize after-tax income and wealth accumulation for individuals or entities.

## Core Competencies

### 1. **Technical Knowledge**
- Understanding of federal, state, and local tax codes
- Knowledge of different tax structures (progressive, regressive, flat)
- Familiarity with tax-advantaged accounts (401(k), IRA, HSA, 529 plans)
- Awareness of deductions, credits, exemptions, and loopholes
- Understanding of capital gains tax, estate tax, and alternative minimum tax

### 2. **Strategic Analysis**
- Income shifting and timing strategies
- Entity selection and business structure optimization
- Investment tax efficiency analysis
- Retirement planning coordination
- Estate and gift tax planning
- Charitable giving strategies
