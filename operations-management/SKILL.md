## Overview
Operations Management (OM) is the administration of business practices to create the highest level of efficiency possible within an organization. It involves converting materials and labor into goods and services as efficiently as possible to maximize profit. This skill encompasses strategic planning, process design, capacity planning, inventory management, quality assurance, and continuous improvement.
