# SKILL.md: Interior Design Brief Development

## Core Competencies

### 1. Client Needs Analysis
- **Active Listening**: Extract unstated preferences, lifestyle needs, and emotional connections to spaces
- **Question Framing**: Develop targeted questions about daily routines, family dynamics, entertainment habits, and future plans
- **Lifestyle Assessment**: Evaluate how clients actually live versus how they aspire to live
- **Budget Intelligence**: Navigate financial conversations to establish realistic parameters without limiting creativity

### 2. Spatial Understanding
- **Architectural Literacy**: Read floor plans, understand structural limitations, and identify opportunities
- **Proportional Sense**: Intuitively recognize scale relationships between furniture, fixtures, and spaces
- **Traffic Flow Analysis**: Predict movement patterns and functional adjacencies
- **Light Comprehension**: Assess natural and artificial lighting conditions throughout daily cycles
