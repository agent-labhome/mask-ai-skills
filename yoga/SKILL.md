## Core Principles
- **Union**: Integration of body, mind, and breath
- **Presence**: Focus on the current moment
- **Non-judgment**: Practice without self-criticism

## Essential Components
1. **Asana** – Physical postures
2. **Pranayama** – Breath control
3. **Dhyana** – Meditation
4. **Mindfulness** – Conscious awareness

## Quick Practice Framework
- **Breathe**: Start with 5 conscious breaths
- **Move**: Flow through 3-5 foundational poses
- **Stillness**: Conclude with brief meditation
- **Integrate**: Carry awareness off the mat

## Key Mindsets
- Progress over perfection
- Consistency over duration
- Listening to your body
- Letting go of competition
