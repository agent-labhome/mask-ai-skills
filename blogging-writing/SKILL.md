## Core Principles
- **Clarity over cleverness** – Write for your reader, not for yourself
- **Value-first** – Every post should solve a problem or answer a question
- **Scannable** – Use headings, lists, and short paragraphs
- **Actionable** – Give readers something to do or think about

## Quick Checklist
- [ ] Strong headline (specific, benefit-driven)
- [ ] Hook in first paragraph
- [ ] One main idea per post
- [ ] Subheadings every 2-4 paragraphs
- [ ] Internal/external links
- [ ] Clear call-to-action
- [ ] Proofread for readability

## Pro Tips
- Write first, edit later
- Use active voice
- Keep sentences under 25 words
- Add visuals when helpful
- Optimize for SEO (keywords naturally)
- Publish consistently
