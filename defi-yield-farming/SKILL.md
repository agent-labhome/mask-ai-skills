## Description
A comprehensive skill for analyzing, comparing, and optimizing yield farming opportunities across various decentralized finance (DeFi) protocols. This skill provides real-time insights into APY/APR rates, risk assessment, gas fee optimization, and protocol comparisons to help users maximize their returns while managing risks in the rapidly evolving DeFi landscape.
