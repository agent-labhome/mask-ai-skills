# User Experience (UX)

## Overview
User Experience (UX) encompasses all aspects of an end-user's interaction with a company, its services, and its products. It focuses on creating meaningful and relevant experiences that meet user needs while achieving business goals.

## Core Competencies

### 1. User Research
- **Methods**: Interviews, surveys, usability testing, field studies, diary studies
- **Analysis**: Affinity mapping, persona development, journey mapping, task analysis
- **Tools**: UserTesting, Lookback, Dovetail, Optimal Workshop

### 2. Information Architecture
- Content organization and labeling
- Navigation design and taxonomies
- Card sorting and tree testing
- Sitemap creation and maintenance
