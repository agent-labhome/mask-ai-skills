## What It Is
A text-to-image AI model that generates detailed images from text descriptions.

## Key Capabilities
- Generate images from text prompts
- Modify images using inpainting/outpainting
- Apply artistic styles
- Create variations of existing images

## Common Use Cases
- Concept art and illustrations
- Design mockups
- Creative exploration
- Image enhancement and editing

## Quick Tips
- Be specific in your prompts (include style, composition, lighting)
- Use negative prompts to exclude unwanted elements
- Experiment with different samplers and settings
- Start simple, then add complexity

## Limitations
- May struggle with precise text rendering
