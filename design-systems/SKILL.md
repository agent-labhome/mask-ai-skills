## Core Competency
Creating comprehensive design system briefs that establish clear foundations, scope, and requirements for systematic design development.

## Skill Level
**Intermediate to Advanced** - Requires understanding of design principles, development workflows, and organizational strategy

## Key Capabilities

### 1. **Strategic Foundation**
- Define design system vision and guiding principles
- Establish business objectives and success metrics
- Identify target users and stakeholder groups
- Align with brand strategy and product roadmap

### 2. **Scope Definition**
- Document current state analysis (audit findings, pain points)
- Define system boundaries and phased approach
- Specify included components, patterns, and templates
- Outline excluded elements and future considerations

### 3. **Technical Requirements**
- Specify platform compatibility (web, mobile, native)
- Define technology stack and dependencies
