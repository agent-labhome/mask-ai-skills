## Purpose
A marketing brief is a strategic document that aligns teams on campaign objectives, target audiences, key messages, and success metrics.

## Core Components

**1. Objective**
- What specific business or marketing goal does this campaign address?
- Example: *Increase trial sign-ups by 25% in Q3.*

**2. Target Audience**
- Primary & secondary personas
- Key demographics, psychographics, and behaviors
- Pain points and motivations

**3. Key Message**
- Single compelling value proposition
- Supporting proof points or differentiators
- Desired emotional response

**4. Channels & Tactics**
- Where will the campaign run? (e.g., social, email, paid search)
- Specific deliverables and formats
