## Purpose
A marketing brief is a strategic document that aligns teams on campaign objectives, audience, messaging, and execution requirements.

## Core Components

**1. Objective**
- What specific business goal does this support? (Awareness, leads, sales, retention)
- How will success be measured? (KPIs: impressions, CTR, conversions, revenue)

**2. Target Audience**
- Primary & secondary personas
- Key demographics, psychographics, pain points
- Current behavior and desired action

**3. Key Message**
- Value proposition (single clear benefit)
- Supporting points (2-3 maximum)
- Tone of voice

**4. Execution Requirements**
- Channels (social, email, paid, content, etc.)
- Assets needed (copy, visuals, video)
- Timeline with key milestones
