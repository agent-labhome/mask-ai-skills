# SKILL.md: Marketing Briefs

## Core Competency
Creating clear, actionable marketing briefs that align stakeholders, define objectives, and guide campaign execution.

## What This Skill Enables
- **Alignment**: Synchronizes marketing, creative, and leadership teams around shared goals
- **Clarity**: Eliminates ambiguity about target audience, messaging, and success metrics
- **Efficiency**: Reduces revision cycles by establishing clear parameters upfront
- **Accountability**: Creates measurable benchmarks for campaign evaluation

## Key Components of a Marketing Brief

### 1. Strategic Foundation
- **Business Objective**: The broader business goal this supports (e.g., increase market share by 5%)
- **Marketing Objective**: Specific, measurable marketing goal (e.g., generate 500 qualified leads)
- **Target Audience**: Detailed persona(s) with demographics, psychographics, pain points
- **Key Insight**: The compelling truth about audience behavior or market opportunity
