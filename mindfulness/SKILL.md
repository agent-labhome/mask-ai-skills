## What It Is
A mental practice of bringing attention to the present moment without judgment.

## Core Principles
- **Awareness**: Noticing thoughts, feelings, and sensations as they arise
- **Acceptance**: Observing without criticism or attachment
- **Presence**: Anchoring attention to the here and now

## Basic Practice
1. Find a quiet space and comfortable position
2. Focus on your breath (or another anchor like sounds or body sensations)
3. When your mind wanders, gently return to your anchor
4. Practice for 5-10 minutes daily

## Benefits
- Reduces stress and anxiety
- Improves focus and emotional regulation
- Enhances self-awareness
- Supports overall well-being

## Quick Tips
- Start small (even 1 minute counts)
- Be kind to yourself when distracted
