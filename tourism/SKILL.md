# SKILL.md: Tourism Brief Development

## Core Purpose
Develop comprehensive, actionable tourism briefs that balance destination appeal with practical implementation strategies.

## Key Competencies

### 1. Destination Analysis
- **Market Positioning**: Identify unique selling propositions (USPs) and competitive advantages
- **Audience Segmentation**: Define target demographics, psychographics, and travel motivations
- **Infrastructure Assessment**: Evaluate existing tourism facilities, accessibility, and capacity
- **Seasonality Analysis**: Map peak/off-peak periods and seasonal opportunities

### 2. Strategic Development
- **Experience Design**: Create compelling visitor journeys and touchpoints
- **Sustainability Integration**: Incorporate environmental, cultural, and economic sustainability
- **Stakeholder Mapping**: Identify and align interests of government, businesses, communities
- **Risk Assessment**: Anticipate challenges (political, environmental, economic)
