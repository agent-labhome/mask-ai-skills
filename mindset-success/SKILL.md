## Core Principles
- **Growth Mindset**: View challenges as opportunities to learn
- **Resilience**: Bounce back from setbacks with purpose
- **Positive Framing**: Reframe obstacles as temporary and solvable
- **Goal Clarity**: Define what success means to you specifically

## Daily Practices
- Morning intention setting
- Gratitude reflection
- Progress tracking (not perfection)
- Constructive self-talk

## Quick Wins
1. Replace \"I can't\" with \"I haven't learned yet\"
2. Celebrate small progress
3. Identify one learning from each challenge
4. Visualize desired outcomes

## Mindset Shifts
- Failure → Feedback
- Problems → Puzzles
- Fixed ability → Developable skills
- Comparison → Personal progress
