## Introduction to Fitness

Fitness is the state of being physically healthy and strong, achieved through regular exercise, proper nutrition, and adequate recovery. It encompasses multiple components including cardiovascular endurance, muscular strength, flexibility, and body composition. This guide provides a structured approach to developing a sustainable fitness practice regardless of your starting point.

## Core Components of Fitness

### 1. **Cardiovascular Endurance**
The ability of your heart, lungs, and blood vessels to deliver oxygen during sustained physical activity.

**Key Activities:**

- Cycling (stationary or outdoor)
- Swimming
- Rowing
- High-Intensity Interval Training (HIIT)
- Dancing or aerobics classes
