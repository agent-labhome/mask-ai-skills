# SKILL: Online Teaching

## Core Competencies
- **Platform Proficiency**: Mastery of tools like Zoom, Google Classroom, Canvas, or Moodle.
- **Digital Content Creation**: Designing engaging presentations, videos, and interactive materials.
- **Virtual Classroom Management**: Facilitating discussions, managing time, and maintaining student focus remotely.
- **Asynchronous Communication**: Providing clear written instructions and feedback.
- **Technical Troubleshooting**: Basic problem-solving for common connectivity and software issues.

## Key Practices
- **Engagement First**: Use polls, breakout rooms, and interactive elements to combat "Zoom fatigue."
- **Clarity & Structure**: Organize content into digestible segments with clear objectives.
- **Accessibility**: Ensure materials are accessible (captions, readable fonts, multiple formats).
- **Presence & Tone**: Project energy and approachability through your voice and on-camera demeanor.
- **Feedback Loops**: Create consistent channels for student questions and formative assessment.
