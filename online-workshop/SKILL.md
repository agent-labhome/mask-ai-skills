# SKILL.md for Online Workshop Brief

## Core Competencies

### 1. **Workshop Design & Development**
- **Learning Objective Crafting**: Ability to create clear, measurable learning outcomes
- **Curriculum Sequencing**: Structuring content from foundational to advanced concepts
- **Activity Design**: Creating interactive exercises that reinforce learning objectives
- **Time Management**: Balancing content delivery with participant engagement
- **Assessment Creation**: Developing methods to measure participant understanding

### 2. **Technical Proficiency**
- **Platform Mastery**: Expertise with Zoom, Teams, Webex, or specialized workshop platforms
- **Digital Tool Integration**: Incorporating tools like Miro, Mural, Google Workspace
- **Troubleshooting**: Basic technical problem-solving for common participant issues
- **Accessibility**: Ensuring content is accessible across devices and for diverse needs
- **Backup Planning**: Having contingency plans for technical failures
