# SKILL: Wellness Brief

## Overview
**Wellness Brief** is a concise daily wellness check-in skill that helps users quickly assess and log their physical, mental, and emotional state. Designed for busy individuals who want to maintain awareness of their wellbeing without lengthy journaling sessions.

## Core Functionality
- **Daily Check-In**: Guides user through 3-4 quick rating questions
- **Mood Tracking**: Simple 1-5 scale ratings for key wellness dimensions
- **Brief Insights**: Provides one personalized observation based on patterns
- **Progress Overview**: Weekly summary of wellness trends

## Interaction Flow
1. **Activation**: User says "Start wellness check" or similar
2. **Quick Assessment**: 
   - Physical energy level (1-5)
   - Mental clarity (1-5)
