# Photo Editing SKILL

## Core Competencies
- **Color Correction**: Adjust white balance, exposure, contrast, and saturation
- **Retouching**: Remove blemishes, objects, and imperfections
- **Cropping & Composition**: Apply rule of thirds, adjust framing
- **Basic Restoration**: Fix old/damaged photos (scratches, fading)
- **File Management**: Understand formats (RAW, JPEG, PNG), resolution, and export settings

## Key Tools
- **Primary**: Adobe Lightroom, Photoshop
- **Alternatives**: Capture One, GIMP, Affinity Photo
- **Mobile**: Snapseed, VSCO, Adobe Lightroom Mobile

## Quick Principles
1. **Subtlety**: Edits should enhance, not overpower
2. **Non-destructive**: Use layers and adjustment layers when possible
3. **Consistency**: Maintain uniform style across a series
