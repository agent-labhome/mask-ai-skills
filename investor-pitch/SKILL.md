## Core Competencies

### 1. Pitch Structure & Content (30%)
**Master the fundamental architecture of a compelling pitch:**
- **Problem Statement:** Articulate a clear, urgent, and sizable market problem
- **Solution:** Present your product/service as the definitive solution
- **Market Opportunity:** Define TAM, SAM, SOM with credible data sources
- **Business Model:** Explain how you make money (unit economics, LTV/CAC)
- **Traction:** Demonstrate validation (revenue, users, partnerships, pilots)
- **Competitive Landscape:** Position yourself with a clear competitive advantage/moat
- **Team:** Showcase why your team is uniquely qualified to execute
- **The Ask:** Specify funding amount, use of funds, and milestones to be achieved

### 2. Storytelling & Narrative (25%)
**Transform data into a compelling narrative:**
- Create emotional connection through customer stories or founder journey
- Structure narrative with clear beginning (problem), middle (solution), end (vision)
- Use analogies to explain complex concepts simply
- Incorporate social proof and validation points strategically
- Maintain consistent theme throughout the pitch

### 3. Financial Acumen (20%)
**Communicate financial understanding confidently:**
- Present clean, logical financial projections (3-5 years)
- Explain key assumptions behind projections
- Articulate unit economics and path to profitability
- Discuss valuation methodology (if appropriate)
- Address capital efficiency and burn rate
