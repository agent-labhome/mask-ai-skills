## Purpose
This document outlines the brainstorming process for generating creative ideas, solutions, and concepts based on a given brief or problem statement.

## Input Requirements
- **Problem Statement**: Clear description of the challenge or opportunity
- **Context**: Background information, constraints, and objectives
- **Target Audience**: Who the solution should serve
- **Success Criteria**: What defines a successful outcome

## Process Flow

### 1. Brief Analysis
- Parse and understand the core problem
- Identify key constraints and requirements
- Clarify ambiguous points through questioning

### 2. Divergent Thinking Phase
- Generate a wide range of ideas without judgment
- Use techniques like:
  - Free association
  - Mind mapping
  - \"What if?\" scenarios
  - Analogical thinking
