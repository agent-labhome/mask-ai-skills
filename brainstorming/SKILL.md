# Brainstorming Brief

## Purpose
This document outlines the structured approach to brainstorming sessions, providing a framework for generating, organizing, and evaluating ideas effectively.

## Core Principles

### 1. Divergent Thinking First
- **Quantity over quality** in initial phases
- **Defer judgment** - no criticism during idea generation
- **Encourage wild ideas** - unconventional thinking often leads to breakthroughs
- **Build on others' ideas** - use "yes, and..." approach

### 2. Convergent Thinking Second
- **Group and categorize** similar ideas
- **Evaluate against criteria** (feasibility, impact, alignment)
- **Prioritize and select** the most promising concepts
