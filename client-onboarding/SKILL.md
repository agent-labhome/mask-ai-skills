## Overview
Client onboarding is the structured process of integrating new clients into your business ecosystem, ensuring they achieve initial value quickly while establishing foundations for long-term success. An effective onboarding process reduces churn, increases lifetime value, and transforms new customers into advocates.

## Business Value Proposition
- **70% reduction** in early-stage churn (industry benchmark)
- **45% increase** in customer lifetime value for properly onboarded clients
- **60% faster** time-to-value realization
- **50% reduction** in support costs during initial engagement period
- Enhanced client satisfaction scores (typically 20-30 point NPS improvement)
