# Client Onboarding - SKILL.md

## Purpose
Efficiently transition new clients from sales to service delivery while establishing clear expectations, building trust, and gathering essential information.

## Core Responsibilities
- **Initial Setup**: Collect client information, configure systems, create accounts
- **Expectation Alignment**: Clarify scope, timelines, communication protocols
- **Relationship Building**: Establish rapport and trust with key stakeholders
- **Knowledge Transfer**: Document client needs, preferences, and context
- **Process Education**: Explain how your organization works and what to expect

## Key Activities
1. **Kickoff Meeting**: Introduce teams, review goals, establish communication cadence
2. **Documentation Review**: Ensure contracts/SOWs are understood and signed
3. **Technical Setup**: Configure tools, access permissions, integrations
4. **Success Planning**: Define metrics, milestones, and success criteria
5. **Handoff to Account Team**: Smooth transition to ongoing relationship management
