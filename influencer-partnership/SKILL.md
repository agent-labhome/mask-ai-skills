## Purpose
To create a clear, structured brief that aligns brand objectives with influencer collaboration, ensuring mutual value and campaign success.

## Core Components

**1. Campaign Overview**
- Objective (Awareness, Consideration, Conversion)
- Key Message & Brand Voice
- Campaign Timeline & Key Dates

**2. Influencer Profile**
- Target Audience Alignment
- Content Style & Aesthetic Fit
- Platform & Format Requirements

**3. Deliverables & Requirements**
- Content Quantity & Type (Reels, Posts, Stories, etc.)
- Mandatory Elements (Hashtags, Tags, Links, Disclosures)
- Usage Rights & Exclusivity

**4. Compensation & Logistics**
- Payment/Fee Structure (Flat, Commission, Product Exchange)
- Product/Service Provision Timeline
