## Core Concepts

### Account Types
- **Checking Accounts**: Daily transactions, debit cards, check writing
- **Savings Accounts**: Interest-bearing, limited withdrawals, emergency funds
- **Money Market Accounts**: Higher interest, check-writing privileges
- **Certificates of Deposit (CDs)**: Fixed term, fixed interest, early withdrawal penalties
- **Retirement Accounts**: IRAs, 401(k)s, tax-advantaged savings

### Banking Operations
- **Deposits**: Cash, checks, electronic transfers
- **Withdrawals**: ATM, teller, electronic transfers
- **Transfers**: Internal (between accounts), external (between institutions)
- **Clearing**: Check processing, settlement periods
- **Reconciliation**: Matching records with bank statements
