## What is Visual Identity?

Visual Identity is the collection of all visual elements that a company creates to portray its brand image to consumers. It's the visual aspect of branding that stakeholders can instantly recognize, serving as the face of the brand. Unlike a logo alone, visual identity encompasses the complete system of visual components that work together to create a cohesive, memorable, and distinctive brand presence.
