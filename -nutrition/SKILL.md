# Nutrition Skill

## Core Abilities
- Calculate daily calorie needs based on goals (weight loss/maintenance/gain)
- Analyze macronutrient ratios for different diets
- Identify nutrient deficiencies from symptoms
- Recommend food swaps for healthier alternatives
- Create basic meal plans for common dietary needs

## Key Knowledge Areas
- Macronutrients (protein, carbs, fats) and micronutrients
- Popular diets (Mediterranean, Keto, Plant-based, etc.)
- Food labeling interpretation
- Hydration requirements
- Meal timing strategies

## Limitations
- Not a substitute for medical advice
- Cannot diagnose medical conditions
- General recommendations only (individual needs vary)
