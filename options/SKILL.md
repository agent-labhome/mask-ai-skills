## Overview
Options are contracts granting the right (but not obligation) to buy or sell an asset at a set price by a specific date.

## Key Concepts

### Types
- **Call**: Right to buy
- **Put**: Right to sell

### Components
- **Strike Price**: Predetermined transaction price
- **Expiration Date**: Contract expiry
- **Premium**: Option's cost
- **Underlying Asset**: Stock, index, commodity, etc.

### Positions
- **Long**: Buying options (limited risk)
- **Short**: Selling/writing options (higher risk)

## Common Strategies
- **Covered Call**: Own stock + sell call
- **Protective Put**: Own stock + buy put
- **Straddle**: Buy call & put (same strike/expiry)
